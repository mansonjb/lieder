The "north" versus "south" distinction on the island largely aligns with the Atlantic (north/west) versus pertuis (east/south) coastline divide.

## North/Atlantic side

Exposed. Wild beaches. Waves. Consistent wind. Surf and kite territory. Le Bois-Plage, La Couarde Atlantic side, Ars coast, Les Portes beaches.

## South/pertuis side

Sheltered. Calm water. Shallow and warm. Ideal for families. Rivedoux, Sainte-Marie, La Flotte, La Couarde south.

## The practical difference

North: you go there for the wind and waves. South: you go there for safe, pleasant family swimming.

## Frequently asked questions

**Which side is better for a family holiday?**
The south (pertuis) side for families with young children. The north gives older children and teenagers more activity.

**Can you easily switch between sides?**
Yes — the island is narrow enough to cycle between them in 15–20 minutes.
