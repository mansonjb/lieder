Is Île de Ré worth it? A direct answer.

## The case for

Outstanding beaches on both coastlines. Beautiful, well-preserved villages. Excellent food and oyster culture. The best flat-cycling landscape in France. The salt marshes and bird reserve. The lighthouse. The pace.

## The case against

Expensive — among the most expensive French beach destinations. Very crowded in July-August. The bridge creates traffic and the feeling of a single-road island in peak season.

## The verdict

For the right visitor at the right time — yes, absolutely. The island's combination of natural beauty, village character and gastronomic identity is hard to find elsewhere on France's Atlantic coast.

At the wrong time (peak August), with wrong expectations (wanting peace and solitude), it can disappoint.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré worth the bridge toll?**
The toll is a small fraction of the total holiday cost. It's not a reason to avoid the island.

**Would I prefer Île de Ré or the Basque coast?**
Entirely different styles. The Basque coast has more dramatic scenery and better surf. Île de Ré has better family beaches, cycling and village life.
