For visitors seeking maximum peace and quiet on the Île de Ré, the western villages are the answer.

## The quietest villages

**Loix:** A peninsula into the marshes, accessible by a single road. No tourist noise. The quietest village on the island by the general consensus of those who know it.

**Les Portes-en-Ré:** The western terminus. No hotel, minimal restaurant. The wild beaches compensate for the limited services.

**Saint-Clément-des-Baleines:** Quiet, small, the lighthouse its only main draw.

## Quietest period

October to April across the whole island. Even Saint-Martin is quiet enough by November.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere quiet on Île de Ré in August?**
Relative to Saint-Martin: Loix and Les Portes are noticeably quieter. Absolute peace in August is not available anywhere on the island.

**Does the western end feel remote?**
Yes — especially Les Portes and the beach area. The distance from the bridge is the effective isolation.
