Île de Ré works differently for families with children versus couples.

## For families

Best villages: La Couarde (both coastlines, central), Le Bois-Plage (Atlantic beaches and camping), Sainte-Marie (calm beaches and kitesurfing for older children).

Best activities: Cycling, pertuis beach days, shore fishing, surf lessons, the lighthouse, nature reserve.

Best months: July and August for full infrastructure. June for uncrowded but full services.

## For couples

Best villages: La Flotte (port atmosphere, intimate restaurants), Ars-en-Ré (authentic, beautiful), Loix (maximum quiet).

Best activities: Slow cycling days, market mornings, good restaurant evenings, sunset at the lighthouse.

Best months: June or September — warm enough for beach, uncrowded enough for romance.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré better for families or couples?**
Excellent for both, at different times and in different villages. Couples do better outside July-August; families thrive in the full season.

**Can couples go without feeling overwhelmed by families in summer?**
Partly. The western end of the island and early mornings maintain a quieter character even in peak season.
