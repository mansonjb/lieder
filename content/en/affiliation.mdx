## Transparency about affiliate links

This guide earns revenue through affiliate partnerships. When you book accommodation through links on this site, we may receive a commission from the booking platform at no additional cost to you.

## Our affiliate partner

The accommodation maps and links on this site use **Stay22**, a booking aggregator that compares rates across platforms including Booking.com, Airbnb, Vrbo, Hotels.com and others. Stay22 may place cookies when you interact with the map widget.

## Editorial independence

Affiliate income does not influence which places we recommend or what we write about them. Recommendations are based on the type of experience each property offers, not on commission rates.

## How to support this guide

Booking through the Stay22 map on any accommodation page supports the costs of maintaining and updating this guide. Thank you.
