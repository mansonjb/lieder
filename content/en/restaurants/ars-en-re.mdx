Ars-en-Ré's restaurant scene is small, local in character and good value — one of the island's most authentic dining experiences.

## What's available

A handful of restaurants in and around the village centre and market square. Seafood and local produce dominate the menus. More relaxed and less formal than the Saint-Martin restaurants.

## The market

The Friday morning market at Ars is excellent — one of the island's most genuinely local. Produce, oysters, bread.

## Seasonal hours

Most restaurants in Ars operate April to October, with limited or no service in winter.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do Ars restaurants accept walk-ins?**
Generally yes outside July-August. In peak season, especially Friday evenings after the market, reserve in advance.

**Are there non-seafood options?**
Yes, though seafood dominates. Salads, grilled meats and vegetarian options are increasingly available.
