Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré has a solid restaurant offer suited to the village's beach-holiday character — reliable without being exceptional.

## What's available

Seafood, crêperies, pizza and casual dining. Good coverage for a week without needing to go elsewhere for every meal.

## Beach snack options

Near Le Gros Jonc, seasonal snack options cater to the surf crowd.

## Best of Le Bois-Plage dining

The island's fresher produce appears everywhere. A good crêpe or simple fish of the day lunch here works well.
