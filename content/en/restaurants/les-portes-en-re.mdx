Les Portes-en-Ré has one or two seasonal restaurants — calling ahead before making a trip specifically for a meal is essential.

## The reality

Minimal restaurant infrastructure in the island's most remote commune. Seasonality is extreme — very limited offer outside July-August.

## What to do

Bring a picnic from Ars-en-Ré market. Self-cater using supplies from the nearest supermarket (Le Bois-Plage or La Couarde).

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere to eat in Les Portes in September?**
Possibly — check in advance. Most places close within weeks of the summer peak.
