For a more ambitious meal on the Île de Ré, a small number of restaurants offer genuinely creative or precise cooking above the standard island offering.

## The upper tier

Saint-Martin-de-Ré and La Flotte have the island's best fine dining options. Quality varies season to season as chefs move; check current reviews before choosing.

## What to expect

Menus built around the best seasonal produce — island potatoes in May, specific fish catches, local salt. Service that takes itself seriously. Wine lists with regional depth.

## Price

Dinner at the island's top tables runs 50–80€ per head for food, more with wine. Lunch set menus at the same restaurants are often 35–50€ — better value.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do the top restaurants on Île de Ré have Michelin stars?**
Historically limited Michelin presence on the island. Check current Michelin listings for any updates.

**Should you book in advance?**
Always, for dinner. For lunch, 2–3 days ahead usually suffices outside July-August.
