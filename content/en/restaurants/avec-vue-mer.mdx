Restaurants with sea views on the Île de Ré are memorable — the combination of good seafood and the water makes for a satisfying meal.

## The best sea-view dining

**La Flotte port** — the most celebrated view on the island. Fishing boats, sea, stone. Lunch in the sun here is hard to beat.

**Saint-Martin quay** — the island's busiest port-side strip. Varied quality; the view is consistent.

**Saint-Clément-des-Baleines area** — near the lighthouse, some seasonal restaurants with Atlantic views.

## Booking

Sea-view tables are the first to be reserved. If this matters, book well in advance and specify your preference.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are sea-view restaurants more expensive?**
Not systematically, though the best-positioned tables may command slight premiums.

**Which is better — port views or ocean views?**
Port views (La Flotte, Saint-Martin) for atmosphere and activity; Atlantic views for the open horizon.
