Beach restaurants (pieds dans le sable — feet in the sand) are limited on the Île de Ré compared to some Mediterranean destinations, but the best pertuis-side options offer a genuine experience.

## What exists

Some seasonal beach bars and casual restaurants operate near the main pertuis beaches. Rivedoux, Sainte-Marie and Le Bois-Plage have the most options.

## What to expect

Simple food — moules frites, fish, salads, crêpes — served with a sea view. Plastic chairs and paper menus. The experience is more about the setting than the gastronomy.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are beach restaurants expensive?**
Similar to island average. The sea-facing premium is modest.

**Do beach restaurants require reservations?**
For busy spots in July-August, arriving by 12:30 or later in the afternoon helps. The best spots fill fast.
