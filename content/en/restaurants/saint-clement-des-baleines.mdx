Saint-Clément-des-Baleines has a very limited restaurant offer — seasonal and small in scale.

## The reality

One or two seasonal options near the village and the lighthouse car park area. Not a dining destination.

## The practical advice

Stock up in Ars-en-Ré (15 minutes by bike) for picnic supplies. Self-cater for most meals if staying in Saint-Clément.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there food near the lighthouse?**
A snack bar near the car park in season.
