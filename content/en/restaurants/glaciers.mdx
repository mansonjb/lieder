Ice cream is one of the island's great summer rituals — a cone from a quality glacier after a beach or market morning.

## The offer

Most villages have at least one artisan ice cream maker in season. Saint-Martin has the widest choice. Quality ranges from artisan sorbet in seasonal flavours to mass-produced cones.

## What to look for

Artisan glaciers using local ingredients: fleur de sel caramel (a natural), seasonal fruit sorbets, flavours that reflect the island's produce.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a particularly good glacier on Île de Ré?**
Recommendations change seasonally as shops open and close. Ask locals for the current favourite.

**Are glaciers open year-round?**
Most operate from May to September.
