Sainte-Marie-de-Ré's restaurant offer reflects its size and family-holiday character — varied, accessible and generally reliable.

## What's available

Seafood restaurants, crêperies, Italian options, casual family dining. Coverage is good for a week's variety.

## Near the beaches

Seasonal snack and restaurant options near Les Grenettes and La Cible cater to the beach crowd.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are Sainte-Marie restaurants good value?**
Generally yes — competitive with the island average, without the Saint-Martin premium.

**Are there restaurants open year-round in Sainte-Marie?**
A limited number. More reliable than the smaller villages in winter.
