La Couarde-sur-Mer's restaurant offer is decent for a central island village — enough variety for a week without repeating.

## What's available

Seafood restaurants, crêperies, a couple of casual options. The market provides good raw ingredients for self-caterers.

## Seasonal

Most restaurants open April to September. Very limited offer in winter.

## Best nearby

For a special dinner, Saint-Martin (20 minutes by bike) and La Flotte (20 minutes by bike) offer more ambitious options.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a good restaurant in La Couarde?**
Competent options exist. Not the island's most celebrated table.

**Are there restaurants near the beach in La Couarde?**
Some seasonal snack options near the Atlantic beach.
