Crêperies are a fixture of the Île de Ré's restaurant scene — casual, affordable and well-suited to families.

## The offer

Both sweet crêpes (dessert, with butter, sugar, jam, chocolate) and savoury galettes (buckwheat, with cheese, ham, eggs, vegetables). A full crêperie meal of one galette and one sweet crêpe runs around 12–18€.

## Where to find them

Every major village has at least one crêperie open in season. Saint-Martin has multiple.

## Who they suit

Families with children (variety of fillings, affordable), cyclists (quick and filling), those who want a lighter meal than a full restaurant service.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are crêperies open year-round?**
Some in Saint-Martin are. Most in smaller villages are seasonal.

**Are crêperies worth eating at on Île de Ré?**
As a practical, affordable meal between activities — yes. Not for a gastronomy experience.
