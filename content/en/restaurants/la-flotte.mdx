La Flotte has the island's best port-side dining — the combination of fresh seafood, harbour views and a beautiful village setting makes it the most satisfying place to eat on the island.

## The port restaurants

Clustered around the harbour square, these restaurants serve what came off the boats. Fish of the day, oysters, moules marinières, shellfish platters. Simple, excellent, memorable.

## The market

Wednesday and Saturday mornings. The best place on the island to eat standing-up oysters with bread and wine.

## Evening dining

La Flotte's evening restaurant offer is smaller than Saint-Martin but more focused. Reserve in advance.

## Frequently asked questions

**Which port restaurant in La Flotte is the best?**
Recommendations change seasonally. All offer similar produce quality — the view and the timing matter as much as the specific table.

**Can you walk in without a reservation in La Flotte?**
For lunch, often yes (arrive early or late). For dinner, reserve in July-August.
