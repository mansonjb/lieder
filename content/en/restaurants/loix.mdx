Loix has minimal restaurant infrastructure — one or two seasonal options at best.

## The reality

A single restaurant, sometimes open, sometimes not. Always check before making Loix your primary lunch destination.

## The alternative

Bring a picnic. The marshes around Loix are one of the island's finest picnic settings.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a café in Loix?**
Sometimes seasonal. Do not rely on it being open without checking.
