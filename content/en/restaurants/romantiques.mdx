The Île de Ré's most romantic restaurant experiences combine good food with beautiful settings — a port view, a candlelit terrace, a quiet village square.

## The best options

**La Flotte port restaurants** — the harbour view at dusk is inherently romantic.

**Saint-Martin quay** — more animated, less intimate, but beautiful.

**Ars-en-Ré** — a quiet square, simpler food, genuinely local.

## Tips

Reserve in advance. Ask for a terrace table. Go slightly off-peak (6pm–7pm before the main rush, or later in the evening when things calm down).

## Frequently asked questions

**What's a romantic dinner budget on Île de Ré?**
50–80€ for two for a good dinner with wine. A lot more at the top tables.

**Is June or September better for a romantic dinner?**
Both are excellent. June has the longest evenings; September has the most beautiful golden light.
