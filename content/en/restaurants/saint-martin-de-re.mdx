Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the island's most varied and concentrated restaurant offer — the best tables require reservations, the casual options are plentiful.

## The port quay

The most famous strip. Varied quality — some excellent, some coasting on location. The best of these set the standard for the island.

## Side streets

Away from the quay, smaller restaurants offer better value with less view. Often more authentic cooking.

## Budget to fine dining

Crêperies and casual sandwiches to creative seasonal menus. The range covers all budgets and occasions.

## Frequently asked questions

**Should you book dinner in Saint-Martin?**
Always in July-August. The best tables fill weeks ahead.

**Is Saint-Martin expensive for restaurants?**
Among the most expensive on the island. Lunch set menus are the most efficient spend.
