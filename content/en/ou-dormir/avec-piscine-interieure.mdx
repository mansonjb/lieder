Indoor swimming pools are rare on the Île de Ré — the island's outdoor-focused culture and mild climate mean most pool options are outdoor.

## What exists

A small number of rental properties have indoor or heated outdoor pools. These are in high demand and priced accordingly.

## Campsites

Some well-equipped campsites have covered pool areas. This is the most reliable option for heated water in shoulder season.

## Off-season swimming

For year-round swimming, the island's limited heated pool offer means this isn't the primary draw.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there public indoor pools on Île de Ré?**
The island has limited public pool infrastructure. Check locally for current options.

**Are private pools in rentals usually heated?**
Some are, some not. Specify when searching — unheated pools in May or October may be cold.
