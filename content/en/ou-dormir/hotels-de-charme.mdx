The island has several notable boutique and characterful hotels — small properties with distinctive design and typically excellent service.

## The best options

Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the highest concentration. Hotel de Toiras is the island's most celebrated. La Flotte has a smaller, quieter alternative. Ars-en-Ré occasionally has chambres d'hôtes that operate at boutique level.

## What to expect

Rooms furnished with care, often in historic buildings. Breakfast typically included. Personal service. Price: generally from €150–250 per room in peak season.

## Booking

These properties have very limited room counts — book 3–4 months ahead for summer, earlier for weekends.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there spa hotels on Île de Ré?**
Some hotel properties have small spa facilities. For a full spa experience, Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the best options.

**Do boutique hotels on Île de Ré accept children?**
Most do, though some smaller properties are better suited to couples. Check before booking.
