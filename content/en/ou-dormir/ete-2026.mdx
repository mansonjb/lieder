## Booking situation

For July and August 2026, most quality properties will be gone by March. If you're planning a summer 2026 visit, the time to book is now or in the coming weeks.

Full island activity: markets, all restaurants open, beaches supervised, water sports at capacity. The island is at its liveliest and most crowded in these two months.

## Beat the peak

Consider late June (23 June–4 July) or early September for summer weather with significantly less crowd and lower prices.
