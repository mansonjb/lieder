Bachelorette parties (EVJF) on the Île de Ré hit a particular sweet spot — beautiful setting, good restaurants, cycling and beach by day, wine and oysters by evening.

## The programme

Arrive Friday. Saturday: market, bikes, Atlantic beach and picnic. Saturday evening: dinner at a port restaurant, wine bar after. Sunday: slow brunch, beach, depart. Adaptable for longer stays with boat trips, kite lessons and other additions.

## Group accommodation

Holiday rentals sleeping 6–10 work well for EVJF groups. Look for properties with outdoor space and a kitchen suitable for group meals.

## Tips

Book restaurants in advance in season. Some venues require groups to pre-order menus.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré popular for EVJF?**
Increasingly so — it has the right combination of beauty, activities and gastronomy without the full party-resort atmosphere.

**Is there nightlife on the island?**
Limited compared to a city. Saint-Martin has a few bars open late. The island's culture is more dinner-and-conversation than clubbing.
