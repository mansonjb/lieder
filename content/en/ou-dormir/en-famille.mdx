Family holidays on the Île de Ré are well-served by the island's infrastructure — cycle paths, calm beaches, good markets, and a wide accommodation offer.

## Best villages for families

La Couarde-sur-Mer (central, two coastlines), Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré (great Atlantic beaches and camping), Sainte-Marie-de-Ré (best services and calm beach). All have the beaches, cycle path access and services that families need.

## Accommodation types

Holiday rentals with gardens work well for families — space, independence and a garden for children. Campsites are excellent value and the best have extensive facilities.

## Practical tips

Book early, confirm pet policy if applicable, check proximity to a supermarket for the daily shop, and look for a cycle path connection to the beach.

## Frequently asked questions

**What age is the island suitable for?**
All ages. The cycle paths and calm beaches work for young children; the water sports and nature activities suit older children and teenagers.

**Are there activities specifically for children?**
Yes — surf lessons, kayak hire, birdwatching tours, the lighthouse, the salt marsh ecomuseum.
