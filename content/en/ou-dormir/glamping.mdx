Glamping (luxury camping) has emerged as an option on the Île de Ré, with a number of properties offering safari tents, cabins and unusual accommodation with camping's outdoor feel.

## What's available

Yurts, safari tents and cabins at a small number of sites. Most offer proper beds, electricity and kitchen access while retaining the outdoor character of camping.

## Where to find it

Search specifically for "glamping île de ré" or filter for "unusual accommodation" on rental platforms. The offer is smaller than the regular camping and rental market but growing.

## Price

Glamping is priced above standard camping but below typical holiday rentals — roughly €100–180 per night in season.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is glamping available year-round on Île de Ré?**
Most glamping properties operate from May to September.

**Is it worth choosing glamping over a rental house?**
For those who want an outdoor-focused holiday with less commitment to a fixed property, yes. Holiday rentals offer more independence and typically more space.
