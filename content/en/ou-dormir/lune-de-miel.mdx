The Île de Ré is an understated and genuinely lovely honeymoon destination — more active and natural than resort-style, better suited to couples who love cycling, good food and beautiful landscapes.

## Why it works

The island's scale is intimate. Villages are walkable. The light in summer is extraordinary. Restaurants are good. The combination of dunes, salt marshes, whitewashed architecture and Atlantic sunsets creates a specific kind of romance.

## Accommodation

Hotel de Toiras in Saint-Martin for luxury. Top-tier chambres d'hôtes in La Flotte or Ars for character. A private house with garden for independence.

## The programme

Do less, better. One village, one market, one beach, one restaurant. Cycle slowly. Watch the sunset from the lighthouse. Eat oysters.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré better for a honeymoon than the Riviera?**
Different. Less glitzy, less hot, more natural. For couples who prefer Atlantic light over Côte d'Azur glamour, it's a genuine alternative.

**When is the island most romantic?**
June, with its long days and flowers; or September, with warm golden light and empty beaches.
