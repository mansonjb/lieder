Staying near a port on the Île de Ré puts you in the social heart of the village — close to restaurants, morning fish markets and the animation that comes with boats.

## The ports to consider

Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the island's main port — large, lively and surrounded by accommodation. La Flotte's smaller port is equally charming, less busy.

## The experience

Eating breakfast with a view of fishing boats returning, watching the comings and goings of pleasure craft, the smell of the sea mixed with café coffee. Port-side accommodation has its own atmosphere.

## Noise considerations

Port areas can be noisy at night in summer. Ask about room orientation if peace is a priority.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is port accommodation more expensive?**
Yes — proximity to the port and its views commands a premium.

**Is Saint-Martin or La Flotte better for port-side accommodation?**
Saint-Martin has more options; La Flotte has more charm in a quieter setting.
