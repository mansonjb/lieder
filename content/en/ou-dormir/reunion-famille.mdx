Family reunions on the Île de Ré work well due to the island's combination of outdoor activities, good food and the universal appeal of a beautiful summer destination.

## Large group accommodation

The island has rental properties sleeping 10–16 for extended family groups. These typically come with large gardens, multiple bathrooms and outdoor dining space. Book 8–12 months ahead.

## Activities for multiple generations

Cycling suits all ages on the flat paths. The market is a communal pleasure. Beaches cover everyone from toddlers (pertuis side) to teenagers (Atlantic and water sports).

## Meals

The island's markets provide outstanding produce for large group meals. One or two restaurant dinners for the group, self-catered other evenings, works well.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there properties sleeping 12 or more on Île de Ré?**
Yes, though the supply is limited and they book out early. Search via local agencies as well as international platforms.

**Is the island suitable for elderly family members?**
The terrain is very flat — easy cycling and walking for those who are mobile. The beaches on the pertuis side are accessible. Some beaches have adapted equipment for reduced mobility.
