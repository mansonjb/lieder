Seafront accommodation on the Île de Ré is available but limited — most properties are set back from the coast in the villages.

## What exists

Some rental houses and gîtes with direct beach access, particularly on the pertuis side. A very small number of hotel properties with sea views.

## The reality

The island's coastal path and village fabric mean most accommodation is a short walk or cycle from the beach rather than directly on it. "Near the beach" (5–10 minutes on foot) is the standard.

## What to look for

Filter for "sea view" or "beachfront" when searching rental platforms. Verify exact distances — descriptions can be creative.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are beachfront properties much more expensive?**
Yes — a significant premium for direct sea access or genuine sea views.

**Is the pertuis or Atlantic side better for sea-view accommodation?**
The pertuis side tends to have calmer views and is more sheltered. The Atlantic side has more dramatic scenery but more exposure.
