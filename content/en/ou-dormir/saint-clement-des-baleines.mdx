Saint-Clément-des-Baleines has minimal accommodation — gîtes and rental properties in a very small village, primarily for those who want to be near the lighthouse.

## What's available

Self-catering gîtes, a handful of rental properties. Nothing resembling a hotel.

## The appeal

Staying near the lighthouse means sunset views and a position at the island's western tip. For the right visitor, this is exactly what they're looking for.

## Alternative base

Ars-en-Ré is 15 minutes by bike and has considerably more accommodation options while offering similar access to the lighthouse and western coast.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere to stay within walking distance of the lighthouse?**
Some properties are very close. Check addresses carefully on the listing platforms.

**How many nights is Saint-Clément worth?**
Two or three nights if you want to properly experience the western end of the island and visit the lighthouse multiple times.
