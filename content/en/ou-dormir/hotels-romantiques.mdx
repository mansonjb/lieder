The Île de Ré has natural romance — the light, the architecture, the coastal walks, the intimate village restaurants. Several properties lean into this.

## Properties for couples

Boutique chambres d'hôtes in La Flotte and Ars-en-Ré with double rooms of genuine character. Saint-Martin's Hotel de Toiras for a luxury couple's escape. Rental cottages in the village centres for self-catering romance.

## What makes it romantic

The island's pace. Cycling to a market, picnicking in the dunes, watching the sun set from the lighthouse. The best romantic experience here is less about the accommodation and more about the programme.

## Tips

Avoid peak-season crowds if romance is the goal — late May, June or September are better than August. Reserve in advance for the restaurants.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré a good destination for a honeymoon?**
Yes — particularly for those who love cycling, beaches and good food rather than resort luxury.

**Are there couples-only guesthouses?**
Some chambres d'hôtes prefer adult guests — check when booking.
