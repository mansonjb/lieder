A romantic weekend on the Île de Ré works best in June or September — warm, light-filled and not overcrowded.

## The programme

Arrive Friday evening via the bridge. Saturday: breakfast at the local bakery, morning at the market (La Flotte or Saint-Martin), afternoon at a quiet beach, dinner at a port-side restaurant. Sunday: cycling through the marshes, sunset from the lighthouse, dinner before the bridge.

## Accommodation for couples

Chambres d'hôtes with character rooms and good breakfast. Boutique hotels in Saint-Martin. A private gîte in La Flotte or Ars-en-Ré with a garden.

## When to go

June: long evenings, flowers in bloom, uncrowded. September: warm, golden light, lower prices, quieter.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré too family-oriented for couples?**
No — while families are numerous in July-August, the island's intimate villages, good restaurants and natural beauty make it equally good for couples, especially in shoulder season.

**What's a romantic dinner option?**
A table at a port-side restaurant in La Flotte or Saint-Martin, reserved in advance. Or a picnic on the dunes at sunset with market supplies.
