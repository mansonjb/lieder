Camping is one of the best-value ways to experience the Île de Ré, and the island has a well-developed campsite infrastructure.

## The offer

Multiple campsites across the island, with the best concentration at Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré, Sainte-Marie-de-Ré and La Couarde-sur-Mer. Facilities range from basic (pitch, water point) to comprehensive (pools, restaurants, entertainment, mobile homes).

## Prices

Pitches from €25–40 per night in peak season. Mobile homes: €100–200 per night in July-August. Both require advance booking.

## Booking

Campsites fill as fast as rental houses for July-August. Book from January onward for peak season.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is camping on Île de Ré good value?**
Yes — the best campsites offer excellent facilities in good locations at a fraction of the rental market price.

**Are there wild camping options?**
No — wild camping is not permitted on the island.
