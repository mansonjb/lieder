Sainte-Marie-de-Ré has the island's largest accommodation stock after Saint-Martin — good availability, wide price range, excellent services.

## What's available

Holiday rentals, campsites, a small number of hotels and chambres d'hôtes. The campsite offer is particularly strong here.

## Why choose Sainte-Marie

Size means more availability. Good supermarket access. Both beach types (pertuis and Atlantic) within easy cycling distance.

## Booking

Standard island advice: January or earlier for July-August. The large stock means more last-minute options than in the smaller villages.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are campsites in Sainte-Marie good?**
Several well-equipped sites with pools and facilities. Among the island's better camping options.

**Is it cheaper than Saint-Martin?**
Generally yes, for equivalent quality.
