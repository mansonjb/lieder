Les Portes-en-Ré has the island's most limited accommodation — gîtes only, no hotel, no campsite. Book many months in advance.

## What's available

Self-catering houses and gîtes in the village. Nothing else. A finite and small stock.

## The experience

Staying in Les Portes is deliberately committing to the island's quietest commune. No restaurant row to fall back on; no hotel bar. The reward is total immersion in the island's quietest, wildest corner.

## Day trip alternative

For those who can't find (or don't want) accommodation in Les Portes, cycling from Ars-en-Ré or Saint-Clément for a day at the beaches is a very reasonable alternative.

## Frequently asked questions

**When should I book Les Portes accommodation?**
January for July-August is not too early. The limited stock means competition is real.

**Is Les Portes a good choice for families with children?**
If the children are happy with beach and cycling and nothing else — yes. Not for children who need organised activities.
