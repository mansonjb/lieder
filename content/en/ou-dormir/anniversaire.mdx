Île de Ré is an excellent setting for a birthday celebration — good restaurants, beautiful settings and the possibility of combining a group stay with quality experiences.

## Group stays

Holiday rentals that sleep 8–12 are available for group birthday celebrations. Look for properties with large gardens, outdoor dining space and multiple bedrooms.

## Restaurants for celebrations

Saint-Martin and La Flotte have the best restaurants for a celebratory dinner. Reserving a table in advance is essential in season; an entire restaurant evening can sometimes be arranged for larger groups.

## What to do

Market morning, beach afternoon, dinner at the port. A cycling day through the salt marshes. A boat trip to Fort Boyard. The combination fills a long weekend with genuinely good experiences.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you book a private boat trip for a birthday group?**
Yes — boat charters for small groups are available from Saint-Martin port.

**What's the best village for a group birthday?**
Saint-Martin for services and restaurants. La Couarde or Le Bois-Plage for space and beach access.
