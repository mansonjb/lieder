Holiday rentals are the dominant accommodation type on the Île de Ré — the vast majority of visitors stay in rented houses or apartments.

## How it works

Typically rented by the week (Saturday to Saturday in season). Houses with gardens, independent access, self-catering. Platforms: Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com, and local island agencies.

## Price range

A house sleeping 6–8 in July-August runs from €1,500–4,000+ per week depending on location, quality and proximity to the beach. Off-season the same house can be €500–900.

## Tips for finding good value

Book early. Consider villages off the main tourist trail (Loix, Saint-Clément). Opt for pertuis-side properties if the Atlantic view isn't essential.

## Frequently asked questions

**Which local agencies handle rentals on Île de Ré?**
Several island-based agencies operate alongside the international platforms. Searching "location île de ré" on Google will show the main local options.

**Is it better to book direct or through Airbnb?**
Direct often saves on platform fees. Use Airbnb or Vrbo to discover properties, then contact owners for direct booking.
