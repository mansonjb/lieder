Budget accommodation on the Île de Ré exists but requires planning — the island is not cheap, but options exist at every level.

## Campsites

The island's best budget option. Well-equipped campsites at Le Bois-Plage, Sainte-Marie and La Couarde provide good value — from around €25–40 per night for a tent pitch, more for mobile homes.

## Off-season rentals

Prices drop 40–60% outside July-August. A property that costs €2,000 per week in August may cost €700 in May or October.

## Villages to choose

The further from Saint-Martin toward the western end, the lower the prices for equivalent properties.

## Frequently asked questions

**What's the cheapest way to stay on Île de Ré?**
Camping in your own tent at a campsite, booked in advance.

**Are there hostels on the island?**
No dedicated youth hostels. The island is not primarily a backpacker destination.
