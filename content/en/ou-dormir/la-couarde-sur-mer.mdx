La Couarde-sur-Mer's central position and family focus make it one of the best-stocked communes for holiday rentals and camping.

## What's available

Holiday rentals (houses with gardens predominate), several well-equipped campsites, limited hotel options.

## The camping offer

La Couarde has some of the island's best-equipped campsites. Good for families who want outdoor living with beach access.

## Pricing

More affordable than Saint-Martin and La Flotte. Good value for what you get.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there last-minute deals in La Couarde?**
More so than in the premium villages, due to larger stock. September and October are particularly flexible.

**Is La Couarde convenient for shopping?**
Yes — the village has good services and the main east-west road runs through.
