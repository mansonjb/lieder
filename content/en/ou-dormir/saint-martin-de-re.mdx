Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the island's widest hotel selection and a premium holiday rental market. If you want a hotel, this is where most of them are.

## Hotels

Boutique hotels within or near the fortifications. Hotel de Toiras is the island's most prestigious. Several 3–4 star properties in and around the citadel walls.

## Other accommodation

Chambres d'hôtes in the historic streets. Large stock of holiday rentals at the island's highest price point.

## Booking

Hotels fill fast for summer and major holidays. For the best properties, book 4–6 months ahead.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there affordable hotels in Saint-Martin?**
The mid-range options exist but fill quickly. Budget accommodation is more limited.

**Is staying within the fortifications worth the premium?**
For most visitors: yes. The combination of location and atmosphere is hard to match.
