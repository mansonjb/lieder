The Île de Ré has a limited but quality hotel offer, concentrated mainly in and around Saint-Martin-de-Ré.

## Where to find hotels

Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the most hotel rooms on the island, from the luxury Hotel de Toiras to 3-star options. La Flotte has a smaller selection. The rest of the island has very few dedicated hotels — most accommodation is in rentals or chambres d'hôtes.

## Price range

Budget hotels: from around €100 per night in July-August. Mid-range 3-star: €120–180. Boutique and luxury: €200+. Prices drop significantly in shoulder season.

## Booking

For the best options in July-August, book 4–5 months ahead. The island's hotel stock is genuinely limited.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there chain hotels on Île de Ré?**
No major chains. The island's hotels are all independent, which is a positive for character and a negative for guaranteed consistency.

**Do hotels include breakfast?**
Some do, some don't. Confirm at booking. Many visitors prefer the local bakeries regardless.
