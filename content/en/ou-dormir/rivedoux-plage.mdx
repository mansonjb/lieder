Rivedoux-Plage is the most accessible village on the island — first off the bridge — and its accommodation reflects that: practical, well-placed and slightly more affordable than the premium villages.

## What's available

Holiday rentals are the dominant offer. Houses and apartments with gardens, often rented by the week. A small number of chambres d'hôtes and serviced apartments.

## Advantages

Easy bridge access, good local services (bakery, pharmacy, supermarket), calm family beaches within walking distance. Lower prices than Saint-Martin for equivalent space.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there hotels in Rivedoux-Plage?**
Limited hotel offer — primarily rentals and chambres d'hôtes.

**Is Rivedoux well-connected for exploring the island?**
Yes — the main cycle path begins here and connects to every other village.
