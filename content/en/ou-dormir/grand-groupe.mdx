Large group accommodation (10+ people) on the Île de Ré requires specific searching, but properties exist.

## What's available

Houses sleeping 10–16 are available on the island, typically with multiple bedrooms, large kitchens and garden dining space. Supply is limited — fewer than 50 genuinely large properties for rent.

## Where to look

Local island rental agencies often have the best large-property inventory. Airbnb and Vrbo have some but the local market has more.

## Booking

Book 9–12 months ahead for July-August. These properties move first.

## Frequently asked questions

**What's the largest group you can accommodate in one rental on Île de Ré?**
Properties sleeping up to 16 exist but are rare. For larger groups, combining two or three adjacent properties is an option.

**Are large group properties more expensive per person?**
Generally cheaper per person than individual hotel rooms, but expensive in absolute terms.
