Chambres d'hôtes (bed and breakfasts) are one of the island's most characterful accommodation options — often in historic stone farmhouses or village homes with personal hosts.

## What to expect

Typically 3–6 rooms in the host's property. Breakfast included. Personal welcome. Often genuinely knowledgeable hosts who know the island well.

## Where to find them

Throughout the island, with concentrations in La Flotte, Saint-Martin, Ars-en-Ré and Le Bois-Plage.

## Booking

Direct with the property via their website or through Booking.com and Airbnb. Many preferred booking periods require minimum stays in peak season.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are chambres d'hôtes more expensive than hotels?**
Often similar pricing. The advantage is the personal service and typically better breakfast.

**Do chambres d'hôtes accept children?**
Most do — confirm when booking as some prefer adult guests.
