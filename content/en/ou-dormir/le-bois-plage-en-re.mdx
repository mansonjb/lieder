Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré has one of the island's strongest camping offers alongside a wide range of holiday rentals, making it one of the best-value bases for families.

## Campsites

Several large, well-equipped sites near the Atlantic beaches. Pools, mobile homes and pitches. Among the island's best camping infrastructure.

## Holiday rentals

Large supply means more competitive pricing than the prestige villages. Good selection of houses with gardens.

## Booking

Campsites and popular rentals book out fast for July-August. Reserve by February at the latest.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Bois-Plage good value for families?**
Yes — the combination of camping infrastructure and rental variety at lower prices than Saint-Martin makes it a strong family option.

**Are there beachfront properties?**
Some — though the Atlantic coast properties are more exposed. Check location carefully.
