La Flotte's accommodation is as characterful as the village — chambres d'hôtes in stone houses, boutique rentals, quality over quantity.

## What's available

Holiday rentals, chambres d'hôtes, a small number of hotel-style properties. The emphasis is on character.

## Demand

La Flotte is one of the island's most sought-after villages. Good properties book out quickly — aim for 6–8 months ahead for peak season.

## Proximity

From La Flotte accommodation, the port, market, beach and restaurants are all walkable. No car needed in-village.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there gîtes for large groups in La Flotte?**
Some, but the village's compact nature means fewer large properties than bigger communes.

**Is La Flotte the most expensive village?**
Among the most expensive, along with Saint-Martin. Quality properties justify their prices.
