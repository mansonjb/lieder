Saint-Martin-de-Ré and La Flotte are the island's two most prestigious village bases, about 7km apart. Choosing between them is one of the most common planning questions.

## Saint-Martin: the case for

More restaurants and bars, more shops, a market that runs most days in season, a UNESCO-listed architectural setting. Nightlife exists (modestly). The most complete urban experience the island offers.

## La Flotte: the case for

More intimate, quieter, arguably more beautiful. The port experience is better (more authentic fishing character). The market (Wednesday and Saturday) is outstanding. The walk to the beach is very short.

## Price comparison

Saint-Martin is marginally more expensive overall. La Flotte premium properties command high prices but the midrange is similar.

## The honest answer

If you want the best of both, base yourself in La Flotte and cycle to Saint-Martin for evenings and days out. It's 15 minutes on flat paths.

## Frequently asked questions

**Which village has better restaurants?**
Saint-Martin for variety; La Flotte for the best individual port-side experience.

**Is La Flotte walkable for all your needs?**
Yes — bakery, market, beach and restaurants are all on foot from any property in the village.
