Choosing which village to stay in on the Île de Ré is the most consequential decision of your trip planning.

## The key factors

1. **How important is the village centre?** If you want restaurants and shops on your doorstep: Saint-Martin or La Flotte. If you're happy to cycle: anywhere.

2. **Beach type preference?** Atlantic (waves, dramatic): Le Bois-Plage, La Couarde north. Calm family swimming: Sainte-Marie, Rivedoux, La Flotte.

3. **Budget?** Higher prices: Saint-Martin, La Flotte. Mid-range: Sainte-Marie, Le Bois-Plage. Lower: La Couarde, Ars, Loix.

4. **Peace and quiet?** East end: livelier. West end: quieter.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there one "best" village on Île de Ré?**
No — it depends entirely on what you want. Saint-Martin is the most complete; it's not the most beautiful.

**Can you visit different villages even if you're based in one?**
Yes — by bike, every village on the island is accessible in a day.
