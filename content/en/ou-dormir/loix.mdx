Accommodation in Loix is genuinely scarce — a handful of gîtes in one of the island's smallest, most remote communes.

## The reality

What exists is small in scale: a few self-catering gîtes, occasionally a chambre d'hôtes. No hotel, no campsite.

## Who comes here

Repeat island visitors who know Loix and specifically want its combination of remoteness and natural beauty.

## Booking

What little exists books early. Use Airbnb, Vrbo or local rental agencies. Check the Stay22 map for current listings.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is accommodation in Loix very expensive?**
Relative to the limited supply, prices can be high in season. The experience justifies it for those who want the quiet.

**If I can't find anything in Loix, where should I stay?**
Ars-en-Ré is nearby (20 minutes by bike) and has significantly more options with a similar natural atmosphere.
