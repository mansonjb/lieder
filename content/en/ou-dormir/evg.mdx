Stag parties (EVG) on the Île de Ré are better suited to groups who want activity and good food over nightlife. The island rewards this.

## Activities

Surfing, kitesurfing, kayaking, cycling challenges (the full island loop), fishing. Saint-Martin's port for evening drinks and dinner.

## Accommodation

Holiday rentals sleeping 8–12, ideally with garden space. Le Bois-Plage or La Couarde for beach and activity access. Saint-Martin for evening options.

## Evening programme

The island's restaurant scene is good enough for memorable dinners. Bar culture is quiet — this is not Ibiza.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré a good EVG destination?**
For activity-focused groups who like to eat and drink well, yes. For those primarily seeking nightlife, it's not the right destination.

**Can you hire a boat for an EVG group?**
Yes — group boat charters from Saint-Martin port are an option.
