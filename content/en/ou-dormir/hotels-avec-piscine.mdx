Properties with pools on the Île de Ré are concentrated in the campsite and premium rental market — hotel pools are relatively rare.

## Campsites with pools

Several large campsites at Le Bois-Plage, Sainte-Marie and La Couarde have well-equipped pool areas. These are the most reliable pool option on the island.

## Rental properties with pools

Private swimming pools are available in higher-end rental houses and gîtes across the island. Filter specifically when searching — "private pool" gives a much smaller subset.

## Hotels

Some hotel properties have small pools. Hotel de Toiras in Saint-Martin has a garden pool.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are pools in rental properties heated?**
Sometimes — check the listing. Unheated pools on the Atlantic coast can be cold, especially before July.

**Do campsites require booking pool access separately?**
No — pool access is typically included in campsite fees.
