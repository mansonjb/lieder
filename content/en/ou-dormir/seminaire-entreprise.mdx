Corporate retreats and seminars on the Île de Ré benefit from the island's combination of a beautiful, distinctive setting and professional facilities in Saint-Martin.

## What's available

Several properties in Saint-Martin and La Flotte have meeting rooms and can host seminars. Some larger rental properties are used for team retreats combining working sessions with outdoor activities.

## Activities for teams

Cycling routes, kayaking, sailing initiation — the island offers strong team building activity options in a genuinely beautiful environment.

## Logistics

The bridge is the only access. Traffic can be an issue in peak season. For seminars outside July-August, the island's peace is an asset.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a conference centre on Île de Ré?**
No dedicated conference centre. Saint-Martin has the largest hotel facilities. Contact the island's tourism office for venue lists.

**What time of year is best for a corporate retreat on Île de Ré?**
May, June, September or October. Peak season (July-August) is too busy and expensive.
