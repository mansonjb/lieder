Full spa facilities are rare on the Île de Ré, but some properties offer treatments and wellness options.

## What's available

A handful of hotel and chambre d'hôtes properties in Saint-Martin-de-Ré and La Flotte offer massage and beauty treatments. Hotel de Toiras has spa services for guests.

## The island context

Île de Ré's appeal is more active — cycling, beaches, nature — than wellness-focused. For a serious spa holiday, La Baule or Royan on the mainland offer more developed options.

## Thalassotherapy

La Rochelle on the mainland has thalassotherapy centres within 30 minutes of the island. An option for day visits.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can non-guests use hotel spa facilities on Île de Ré?**
Generally not — spa services tend to be for hotel guests only. Check with individual properties.

**Is there a thalassotherapy centre on the island?**
Not as a dedicated centre. Closest is on the mainland at La Rochelle.
