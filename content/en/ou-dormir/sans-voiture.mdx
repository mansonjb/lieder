Île de Ré is genuinely one of France's best car-free holiday destinations. The cycle path network means a car is optional for most visitors.

## How it works

From La Rochelle, the Lilas bus serves the island's main villages (seasonal schedule). From Rivedoux, cycle hire gets you everywhere.

## Best villages without a car

Saint-Martin-de-Ré and La Flotte have the most services on foot. La Couarde and Le Bois-Plage are good cycling bases. Rivedoux is the bridge arrival point.

## Accommodation tips

Choose a village with walkable services (bakery, market, restaurant). Confirm your property has secure bike storage or hire a bike from the village.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you explore the whole island without a car?**
Yes — a week of cycling the island is completely doable. Even a day gives you a large portion of it.

**Does the Lilas bus go to all villages?**
The main villages along the road. The most remote communes (Loix, Les Portes) are less well-served.
