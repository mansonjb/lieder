Finding pet-friendly accommodation on the Île de Ré requires specific filtering — many properties don't accept animals, but good options exist.

## How to search

Use "pets welcome" or "animaux acceptés" filters on Airbnb, Vrbo and Booking.com. Always confirm directly with the property before booking.

## Beach rules

Dogs are banned from most beaches from May to September. This limits what you can do with your dog during the main beach season. Off-season, dogs are welcome on most beaches on a lead.

## What to expect

Pet-friendly properties typically charge a cleaning supplement. Some have size restrictions (dogs under 15kg, for example). Confirm all terms before booking.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there dog-friendly hotels on Île de Ré?**
Some accept dogs — check specifically as policies vary. Smaller properties and chambres d'hôtes tend to be more flexible.

**Where can you walk a dog in season?**
Cycle paths, village streets and the coastal trails away from supervised beaches. The island is manageable with a dog outside the main beach areas.
