At the top end of the island's accommodation market, a small number of properties offer genuinely luxurious experiences.

## The flagship

Hotel de Toiras in Saint-Martin-de-Ré is the island's most prestigious hotel — a 17th-century mansion turned 5-star property, with exceptional service, garden and pool. It represents the ceiling of what the island offers in hotel terms.

## Other premium options

Several chambres d'hôtes and private rental properties reach luxury standard. The best of these combine historic architecture, private gardens and pool.

## Price

Hotel de Toiras rates typically start from €300+ per night in season. Premium rental properties start from €300–500/week at the low end to several thousand for the best.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a genuine luxury experience on Île de Ré?**
Yes — Hotel de Toiras is a serious luxury property. Beyond that, the island's charm is genuine but its luxury infrastructure is limited.

**Do luxury properties on Île de Ré have private pools?**
Some rental properties do. Hotels more rarely — pool access is often communal.
