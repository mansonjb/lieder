A two-day cycling itinerary covering the island from east to west and back, using the main cycle paths.

## Day 1 (east to central-west)

Start at Rivedoux-Plage (bridge). Cycle along the pertuis coast to La Flotte. Stop for coffee at the port. Continue to Saint-Martin-de-Ré for a quick rampart walk. Head north-west through the marshes toward La Couarde. Afternoon swim at the Atlantic beach. Night: La Couarde or Ars-en-Ré.

Total cycling: approximately 30km.

## Day 2 (west and return)

Morning: Ars-en-Ré to Loix loop through the marshes. Return to Ars, then west to Saint-Clément and the Phare des Baleines. Option to push on to Les Portes (5km more). Return via the central route to Rivedoux. Total: approximately 40km.

## Electric bike option

The same itinerary on electric bikes reduces effort significantly. Recommended for those who want to cover the distance comfortably.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is 70km over two days manageable for a moderate cyclist?**
Yes — the flat terrain makes it much easier than the same distance on hilly ground.

**Where can you leave luggage while cycling?**
Your accommodation. Most cyclists do a round trip from a single base; touring cyclists use panniers.
