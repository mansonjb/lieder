Ten days on the Île de Ré is for those who want to settle in fully — or who are combining the island with the La Rochelle region.

## How to use 10 days

Days 1–7: the full island week (see the one-week guide).

Days 8–10: deeper exploration and integration.
- A second visit to Ars or Loix at a different time of day.
- A full day in La Rochelle (30 minutes from the bridge — aquarium, old port, markets).
- A boat trip to Île d'Aix or Île d'Oléron.
- A day doing nothing at a specific beach.

## The value of longer stays

After 7 days you start recognising faces at the bakery. You know which beach is best at which time. You stop planning and start living the island.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does the island get boring after a week?**
Not for those who enjoy cycling, beaches, good food and peace. For those who need constant novelty, it may.

**Is La Rochelle worth a day trip from Île de Ré?**
Yes — the old port, the towers, the aquarium and the food market make for an excellent day.
