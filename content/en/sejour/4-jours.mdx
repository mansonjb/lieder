Four days is enough to explore the island fully without rushing, including the quieter western villages.

## Four-day structure

**Day 1:** Eastern end — Rivedoux, Sainte-Marie, La Flotte. Market and beach.

**Day 2:** Saint-Martin-de-Ré — ramparts, museum, port, dinner.

**Day 3:** Central and west — Le Bois-Plage beaches, La Couarde, Ars-en-Ré salt marshes.

**Day 4:** Far west — Saint-Clément, Phare des Baleines, Les Portes wild beaches. Long cycling day or electric bike recommended.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you cycle the full island in 4 days without being exhausted?**
Yes — breaking it into sections as above is comfortable for moderate cyclists.
