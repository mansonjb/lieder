Three days allows you to slow down and see the island without rushing.

## Three-day structure

**Day 1:** Saint-Martin-de-Ré. Ramparts, museum, port, market. Dinner in town.

**Day 2:** Cycle to La Flotte for the morning market. Continue to Ars-en-Ré via the salt marshes. Lilleau des Niges reserve if time. Dinner in Ars.

**Day 3:** Atlantic beaches at Le Bois-Plage. Afternoon: Phare des Baleines (lighthouse). Return via the island's west coast.

## What this covers

The main historic site, the prettiest village, the nature highlights, the lighthouse and the best beaches.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is 3 days too short to feel like you've been to Île de Ré?**
You'll feel you've been — but not that you've settled in. A week is what it takes to feel like a temporary local.
