A week on the Île de Ré is the island's natural unit — enough time to explore thoroughly, slow down, develop rhythms and leave satisfied.

## The week's shape

Days 1–4: systematic exploration, one main area per day (eastern coast, Saint-Martin, central and marshes, western tip).

Days 5–7: settling in. Revisit your favourite places. Longer at a specific beach. A boat trip. A market day as event rather than agenda item.

## What a week covers

Every village, both coastlines, the lighthouse, the nature reserve, the salt marshes, the best markets, the best restaurants. The island at a pace that feels right.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is a week long enough to do everything?**
Yes. A week is the ideal duration.

**Should you move accommodation during a week?**
Most visitors base in one place. Moving wastes time and energy that's better spent on the island.
