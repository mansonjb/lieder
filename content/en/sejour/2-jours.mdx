Two days on the Île de Ré cover the essentials well — both coastlines, the main towns, a taste of nature and good food.

## Day 1

Morning: Saint-Martin-de-Ré ramparts and market. Lunch at a port restaurant. Afternoon: cycle to La Flotte (15 minutes), explore the port and streets. Evening: dinner at La Flotte.

## Day 2

Morning: Cycle west through the salt marshes — La Couarde, toward Ars-en-Ré. Market stop if it's the right day. Afternoon: Atlantic beach at Le Bois-Plage or Le Gros Jonc. Return. Sunset from the highest point you can find.

## What you'll have covered

The island's main town, its most beautiful village, an Atlantic beach, the salt marsh landscape, a market.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is 2 days enough to understand the island?**
For a first impression, yes. You'll leave wanting to come back for longer.
