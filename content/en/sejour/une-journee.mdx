A well-structured day on the Île de Ré gives a genuine introduction to the island.

## The programme

**Morning:** Cross the bridge and head to La Flotte for the market (Wednesday or Saturday) or a coffee at the port. Cycle to Saint-Martin-de-Ré for the ramparts (20 minutes on flat paths).

**Midday:** Lunch in Saint-Martin — set menu at a port restaurant, or oysters at the market.

**Afternoon:** Cycle west to Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré (30 minutes) for the Atlantic beach. Swim. Return via the cycle path.

**Evening:** If staying, dinner at La Flotte or Saint-Martin. If returning to La Rochelle, cross the bridge before the evening peak.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is a day trip from La Rochelle worth it?**
Yes — a well-structured day gives a real impression of the island.

**Can you hire a bike for a day visit?**
Yes — hire shops in every village, including Rivedoux immediately off the bridge.
