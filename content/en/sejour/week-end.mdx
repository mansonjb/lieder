A weekend on the Île de Ré (2–3 nights) works well as a getaway, but requires focused planning to avoid spending the entire time in transit and crowds.

## Arriving smart

Avoid arriving Saturday at noon in summer — the bridge traffic and rental changeover make this the worst time. Friday evening arrival or Sunday mid-morning avoids the worst of it.

## The weekend programme

**Friday evening:** Arrive, check in, dinner at your village.
**Saturday:** Market (La Flotte or Saint-Martin), cycle day, beach afternoon, dinner reserved in advance.
**Sunday:** Morning at the lighthouse or Ars-en-Ré, departure before 15:00 to beat the Sunday return traffic.

## When weekends work best

June and September weekends are ideal — full services, no peak-season crowd intensity.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is a weekend on Île de Ré worth the bridge toll?**
Yes — the toll (€16–19) is a fraction of the accommodation cost. Don't let it be a deciding factor.

**What's the worst time for a weekend visit?**
A July or August long-weekend Saturday. Traffic, crowds and stress. Friday evening arrival changes everything.
