Five days allows real rhythm to develop — favourite beaches revisited, a second market morning, a slower day built in.

## Five-day structure

Use the 4-day plan as the base, adding a fifth day for:
- A slow morning at your favourite beach
- A boat trip to Fort Boyard or Île d'Aix
- Revisiting the market at a different village
- A cycling day specifically focused on birdwatching (Lilleau des Niges and the marshes)

## The rhythm

By day 5, you know your bakery, your preferred beach time, your favourite sunset spot. This is when the island starts to feel like yours.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is 5 days better than a weekend at the same price?**
Yes — for Île de Ré specifically, longer stays extract far more value per euro spent.
