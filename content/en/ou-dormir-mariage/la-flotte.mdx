La Flotte is one of the island's most visually appealing settings for a wedding or wedding weekend — its port, market and historic streets provide a ready-made backdrop.

## Why La Flotte for a wedding

One of France's most beautiful villages. The port-side restaurants are excellent for rehearsal dinners and wedding brunches. The market on Wednesday and Saturday mornings is a perfect activity for guests.

## Accommodation

Quality chambres d'hôtes and rental properties in the village. A good-character base for the wedding party. Guests can stay in La Flotte or spread across neighbouring communes.

## Venues

Some rental properties and local venues can accommodate ceremonies and receptions. Ask the local mairie about civil ceremony requirements.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Flotte better than Saint-Martin for a wedding?**
More intimate, less busy. Saint-Martin has more services; La Flotte has more charm.

**Can the port-side restaurants accommodate a wedding dinner?**
Some can handle groups of 20–30. Larger groups may require exclusive hire — enquire directly with the restaurant.
