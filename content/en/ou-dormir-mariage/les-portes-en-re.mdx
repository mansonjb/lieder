Les Portes-en-Ré for a wedding is for those who specifically want the island's wildest, most remote setting and have accepted the limited services that come with it.

## The appeal

Extraordinary wild beaches. Total quiet. A wedding here feels genuinely off the beaten track.

## The challenge

Minimal restaurant infrastructure. No hotel. Very limited accommodation. A wedding at Les Portes requires careful logistics — either full self-catering or bringing everything in.

## Who it suits

Intimate weddings (under 20 people) where the couple specifically wants the island's most remote character and is happy to manage the logistics.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Les Portes practical for a large wedding?**
No — the infrastructure simply isn't there. For larger weddings, Saint-Martin or La Flotte are more viable.

**Is there a venue in Les Portes?**
Not a formal venue. Private rental properties with gardens are the only option.
