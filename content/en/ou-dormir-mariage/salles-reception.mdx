Reception venues on the Île de Ré are not as numerous or formal as on the mainland, but specific options exist for larger celebrations.

## What's available

Private rental properties with gardens for outdoor receptions. Some restaurants can be exclusively hired for evening events. A small number of dedicated event spaces.

## The island approach

The most successful Île de Ré weddings typically don't follow the formal "hired ballroom" model — they use a beautiful rented property, a good caterer and the island's natural setting.

## Finding a venue

Contact island wedding planners or the tourism office for venue referrals. Search specifically for "lieu de réception île de ré" alongside local agency contacts.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a formal wedding venue with capacity for 100+ guests?**
Options are limited for very large guest lists. Most island venues work best for 40–80 people outdoors.

**Can a restaurant be hired exclusively for a wedding dinner?**
Some restaurants accommodate this for evening bookings. Negotiate directly and well in advance.
