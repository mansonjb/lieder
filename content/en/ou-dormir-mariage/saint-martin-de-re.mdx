Saint-Martin-de-Ré is the island's most complete wedding base — the widest range of restaurants, the most hotel rooms, and the most iconic setting.

## Why Saint-Martin for a wedding

The Vauban fortifications, the UNESCO-listed town, the harbour, the restaurants — Saint-Martin has the most complete infrastructure for weddings. Hotel de Toiras is the natural choice for the bridal party.

## Guest accommodation

The island's highest density of hotel rooms. The best choice for guests who want hotel accommodation rather than rentals.

## Venues

Some properties within and near Saint-Martin accommodate receptions. The town hall (mairie) in Saint-Martin handles civil ceremonies for the commune.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Hotel de Toiras a wedding venue?**
It accommodates wedding parties and can facilitate celebrations for guests. Contact directly for specifics.

**What's the best restaurant for a wedding dinner in Saint-Martin?**
Several restaurants can accommodate groups of 20–40. La Baleine (if still operating) and similar port-side establishments are the most cited. Reserve 6–12 months ahead.
