Loix for a wedding or celebration offers the island's most intimate and remote atmosphere — beautiful marshland setting, absolute quiet, but minimal services.

## The setting

Salt marshes, egrets, narrow lanes and utter peace. The village's character is powerful if you know what you're looking for.

## Practical reality

Very limited accommodation, no real restaurant infrastructure, no hotel. Loix works for small gatherings where guests are self-sufficient.

## Best use

As the venue for part of a wedding weekend — a cycle from a larger nearby village, a picnic in the marshes, a slow afternoon. Not as the primary base for a large guest group.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you have a wedding reception in Loix?**
Only with complete self-catering at a rental property. No venue infrastructure exists.

**How many guests is realistic for a Loix-based wedding?**
Up to about 15–20, if accommodation is found. Beyond that, logistics become very difficult.
