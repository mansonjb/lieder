The most sought-after wedding venues on the Île de Ré are those that combine ceremony and reception space with on-site accommodation — limiting the logistics of transport.

## What exists

A number of large private rental properties with gardens, terraces and sufficient bedrooms accommodate the wedding party under one roof. These range from converted farmhouses to contemporary houses with sea views.

## How to find them

Search via island wedding planners and specialist rental agencies. Terms to search: "propriété avec chambres," "location mariage île de ré."

## What they offer

Typically: 6–12 bedrooms, large garden and terrace, full kitchen. Some have pools. Ceremonies can be held in the garden; dinners catered on-site.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there château-style wedding venues on Île de Ré?**
Not formal châteaux. The island's vernacular architecture is more farmhouse and village house. The aesthetic is Atlantic rather than château-classical.

**How far in advance should we book a wedding property?**
12–18 months for summer. The best properties have multi-year waitlists.
