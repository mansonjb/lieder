Accommodating wedding guests on the Île de Ré requires planning — hotel stock is limited but quality options exist.

## The hotel situation

Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the most hotel rooms on the island. Hotel de Toiras at the luxury end, several 3-star properties for the mid-range. La Flotte has a smaller selection.

## Group booking

Some hotels accept group bookings for wedding parties. Enquire directly well in advance — a year or more for summer dates.

## Mixed accommodation strategy

Bridal party in a premium chambre d'hôtes or rental; immediate family in a rental house; other guests in the nearest available hotel. This works well on the island's scale.

## Frequently asked questions

**How many hotel rooms are available near Saint-Martin-de-Ré?**
The island has limited hotel stock overall — probably 200–300 rooms across all properties. Large weddings (100+ guests) will need a mixed accommodation approach.

**Should we reserve a hotel block for guests?**
Contact hotels directly to discuss group reservation terms. Lead time of 12+ months is recommended for summer.
