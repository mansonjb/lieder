Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré offers wedding and celebration stays with the island's best Atlantic beach access — ideal for couples and guests who want a beach-focused celebration.

## The setting

Central on the island, with excellent cycle connections and the island's finest Atlantic beaches immediately accessible.

## Accommodation for wedding parties

Large rental properties with gardens for the wedding party. Guest overflow into the village's rental and camping market.

## Celebrations

The beach is a natural venue for informal elements of a wedding weekend — sunrise walks, group surf lessons, beach picnic. The village's restaurants can accommodate groups.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Bois-Plage suitable for a formal wedding reception?**
Better for informal, activity-focused celebrations. Saint-Martin or La Flotte have more formal dining infrastructure.

**What activities can wedding guests do in Le Bois-Plage?**
Surfing, cycling, beach, markets. Good for an active group.
