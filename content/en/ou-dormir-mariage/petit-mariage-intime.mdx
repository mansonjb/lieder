The Île de Ré is perfectly scaled for an intimate wedding — a small gathering where the island itself becomes part of the experience.

## The concept

A wedding for 10–25 people, based in a rented village house or chambre d'hôtes, with the celebration woven into the island's rhythms: market morning, beach afternoon, port-side dinner.

## Best villages

La Flotte or Ars-en-Ré for atmosphere and beauty. The accommodation scale suits small groups perfectly.

## Programme

Friday arrival and dinner. Saturday: market, beach, late afternoon ceremony, dinner at the port. Sunday: brunch, departure. Two nights, unforgettable.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is an intimate wedding cheaper on Île de Ré?**
Significantly cheaper than a formal large wedding — the island's character does the work, reducing the need for elaborate decoration.

**Can a civil ceremony be conducted on the island?**
Yes — contact the commune's mairie well in advance to understand the administrative requirements.
