This guide to the Île de Ré is produced independently, without sponsorship from local tourist boards or hoteliers. Its goal is to be useful — clear, honest and practical — for visitors planning a trip to the island.

## What this guide covers

Every village on the island, all major beaches, accommodation options across every budget, restaurants by type and location, practical travel information, seasonal advice and what to do on the island beyond sunbathing.

## Affiliate links

This site uses Stay22 affiliate links for accommodation recommendations. When you book through these links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. It doesn't affect which properties we recommend — we only link to places we'd genuinely suggest.

## How the guide is organised

Pages are organised by topic: villages, beaches, accommodation, restaurants, activities, and practical travel planning. Each village has its own section covering where to stay, eat and what to do locally.
