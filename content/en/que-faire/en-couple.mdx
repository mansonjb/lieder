The Île de Ré is well-suited to couples — the scale is right, the restaurants are good, the cycling is excellent and the beaches beautiful.

## The couple's programme

Morning: slow breakfast from the bakery, eaten on the terrace. Late morning: cycling toward Loix through the marshes. Lunch: oysters at a port-side restaurant. Afternoon: beach and reading. Evening: dinner reserved in advance at a good restaurant in La Flotte or Saint-Martin.

## Best villages for couples

La Flotte and Ars-en-Ré for atmosphere. Loix for pure quiet. Saint-Martin for services and evening life.

## Best season

June or September: warm, uncrowded, long evenings.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré romantic?**
The setting, pace and food combination is genuinely romantic. Less so in the peak of August when crowds arrive.

**What's a memorable couple's activity?**
Cycling to the lighthouse at sunset and climbing for the view. Dinner at a La Flotte port restaurant. A morning at the La Flotte market followed by a picnic on the beach.
