Sailing and catamaran hire on the Île de Ré make use of the island's consistent Atlantic wind and protected pertuis waters.

## What's available

Catamaran hire and sailing lessons from several providers in season, primarily from Saint-Martin-de-Ré port. Options range from a full sailing course to a half-day charter.

## Conditions

The pertuis is a sheltered sailing ground with good consistent wind. The open Atlantic is available for more experienced sailors.

## Boat trips

Several operators run day trips and half-day excursions — to Fort Boyard, Île d'Aix, or simply along the island's coast. These require no sailing skill.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do you need sailing experience to hire a catamaran on Île de Ré?**
For unsupervised hire, some level of competence is typically required. For supervised courses and guided trips, no experience is needed.

**Are there family sailing trips available?**
Yes — guided boat trips operate for all ages with no sailing requirement.
