The island has a long list of genuinely free experiences — the best things to do here don't require an entry fee.

## Free highlights

- Walking the Vauban ramparts in Saint-Martin (always open, free)
- Cycling the full island on the free-to-use path network
- All the beaches
- The Abbaye des Châteliers ruin (open access, free)
- Markets (free to browse, pay for what you buy)
- Birdwatching at Lilleau des Niges (free path access)
- Watching the sunset from anywhere on the western coast
- The Loix marsh cycling loop
- Shore fishing at low tide (pêche à pied)

## What costs money

Climbing the lighthouse (entry fee). Museum visits. Guided tours. Water sports hire and instruction.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you spend a good week on Île de Ré without spending much?**
Yes — camping accommodation, a bike, and the free activities above make for an excellent and affordable week. Add market food and basic restaurants and the budget stays manageable.

**Are the cycle paths free?**
Yes, completely free to use.
