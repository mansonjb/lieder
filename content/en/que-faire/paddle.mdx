Stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) has become one of the island's most popular water activities, particularly on the calm pertuis coast.

## Where to paddle

The pertuis side is ideal — shallow, calm, clear water in good conditions. Popular spots: Sablanceaux, Les Grenettes, the calm bays near La Flotte.

## Hire

Stand-up paddleboards available for hire at several beaches in season. Typically €15–25 per hour. Some providers offer lessons for beginners.

## Conditions

Best in calm weather with light wind. The Atlantic side is for experienced paddlers; the pertuis side works well for beginners.

## Frequently asked questions

**How difficult is stand-up paddleboarding?**
Easier than it looks. Most beginners are upright and paddling within 10–15 minutes.

**Can you take a paddleboard on the tidal channels near the marshes?**
Yes, at high tide. The narrow channels and bird life make for an excellent experience.
