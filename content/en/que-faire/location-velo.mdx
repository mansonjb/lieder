Hiring a bike on the Île de Ré is simple, well-organised and accessible in every village.

## Where to hire

Hire shops exist in every major village — Rivedoux, Sainte-Marie, La Flotte, Saint-Martin, Le Bois-Plage, La Couarde, Ars-en-Ré. Multiple competing providers means reasonable prices.

## Types available

Standard bikes (adults and children), tandem bikes, trailers for small children, cargo bikes for families, and — increasingly — electric bikes. Children's bikes from about age 3 (with pedal assist).

## Prices

Standard bike: approximately €10–15 per day. Electric bike: €25–35 per day. Weekly rates are proportionally cheaper. Book electric bikes in advance — they go fast.

## Returning your bike

Most hire agreements are same-village return. If you want to ride one-way (east to west, for example), ask the hire shop whether they offer collection or multi-point return.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do you need to book in advance?**
For July-August, especially electric bikes: yes. Book a day or two ahead for standard bikes in peak season.

**Can you hire a bike without a helmet?**
Helmets are provided or available for children. Adults are not legally required to wear one on cycle paths in France.
