The northern route from Saint-Martin to the Phare des Baleines follows the island's wild western coast, passing through Loix, Ars-en-Ré and Saint-Clément.

## The route

Saint-Martin → La Couarde → through the marsh loop to Loix → back to Ars-en-Ré → west to Saint-Clément → Phare des Baleines.

## Distance and difficulty

Approximately 35 km one way. Return the same or via the central axis. An easy day with an electric bike; a good half-day for a fit cyclist.

## Highlights

The Loix marsh loop is one of the island's most beautiful cycling sections. Ars-en-Ré for lunch or coffee. The lighthouse itself for the obvious reason.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you extend this route to Les Portes?**
Yes — Les Portes is a further 5 km west from Saint-Clément. Makes for a full day.

**Is the section through the marshes to Loix flat?**
Very flat. The route passes through salt pan landscapes with wide horizons and abundant birds.
