The full island loop is the Île de Ré's most ambitious cycling day — approximately 65 kilometres covering every commune from the bridge to the lighthouse and back.

## The route

Start at Rivedoux (bridge). Head east along the pertuis coast to La Flotte and Saint-Martin. Turn north and west to Loix peninsula, then back south to La Couarde. Continue west through Le Bois-Plage, Ars-en-Ré, Saint-Clément and Les Portes. Return via the central route.

## Difficulty

Flat throughout — the island has virtually no elevation. The distance is the challenge. A fit cyclist can complete it in 5–6 hours of riding, allowing for stops.

## With electric bikes

The tour becomes comfortable for most adults with an electric bike. Allow 6–7 hours including stops, lunch and sightseeing.

## Best season

June or September. July-August the paths are crowded and heat can be intense.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do you need to be a serious cyclist to do the full tour?**
Not a serious cyclist, but reasonably fit. A full day of cycling on an electric bike is manageable for most active adults.

**Where should you stop for lunch?**
Ars-en-Ré or Saint-Martin-de-Ré depending on your route and timing. Both have good options.
