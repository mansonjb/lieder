The salt marsh cycling route is one of the island's most rewarding for landscape and tranquility — flat, wide-skied and full of birds.

## The route

La Couarde → south along the marsh edge → loop through Loix → return via Ars-en-Ré.

## Distance

Approximately 20–25 km, a comfortable half-day.

## What you'll see

Active salt pans (in summer), egrets and herons working the mudflats, the flat luminous landscape that characterises this part of the island. The Loix village centre for a stop.

## Best time

Morning for best light and bird activity. July-September for active salt harvesting.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is this route suitable for beginners?**
Yes — flat, on dedicated cycle paths, not long.

**Is there anywhere to stop for refreshments?**
Loix has very limited café options. Bring water and snacks or plan your stop in Ars-en-Ré.
