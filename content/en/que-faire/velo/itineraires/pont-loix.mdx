The route to Loix from the main island passes through some of the most distinctive landscape on the island — a thin road across salt marshes with water on both sides.

## The route

La Couarde → north through the marshes → Loix peninsula → return the same way or loop via the coast.

## Distance

About 12–15 km return. Short enough to be a half-morning ride.

## The experience

Cycling the causeway to Loix is one of the island's most memorable short rides. On either side, salt pans and mudflats; birds working the margins; the village appearing at the end of a flat road with nothing behind it.

## Combine with

Shellfish gathering at Le Boutillon if the tide is right. Lunch at Ars-en-Ré on the way back.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is the road to Loix shared with cars?**
Yes, it's a single road. Dedicated cycle path runs alongside for much of it. Traffic is light.

**Is this route suitable for very young children?**
Yes — flat, short, interesting destination.
