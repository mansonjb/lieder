The southern coast route follows the pertuis shore from Rivedoux through the village chain to Le Bois-Plage, connecting the island's best family beaches.

## The route

Rivedoux → Sainte-Marie-de-Ré → La Flotte → Saint-Martin → Le Bois-Plage south coast.

## Distance

Approximately 25 km. Easy half-day with frequent stops.

## What you'll see

Sablanceaux beach, Les Grenettes, La Flotte port and beach, Saint-Martin port. A good overview of the pertuis coast's character.

## Best for

Families wanting to sample multiple calm beaches in one day.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you stop and swim at multiple beaches on this route?**
Yes — the route passes directly by or within short distance of several supervised pertuis beaches.

**Is this route good for children?**
Excellent — flat, on dedicated paths, with interesting stops along the way.
