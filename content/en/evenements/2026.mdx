A guide to events and seasonal highlights on the Île de Ré in 2026.

## The recurring calendar

**April-June:** Season opening, spring markets begin, bird migration, first AOP potatoes.

**July-August:** Full season. All events, markets, festivals. Bastille Day (14 July) celebrated in Saint-Martin.

**September-October:** Salt harvest begins, autumn bird migration, markets continue into early October.

## Specific 2026 events

Specific programming for 2026 is subject to confirmation. Check the Île de Ré tourism office website for current event listings.

## What's reliably worth attending

The La Flotte market (Wednesday and Saturday mornings) runs throughout the season. The Ars-en-Ré Friday market is excellent in summer. The Saint-Martin market runs most mornings in season.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a festival on Île de Ré in 2026?**
The island hosts various events throughout the season. Confirm with the tourism office for 2026 specifics.

**Is Bastille Day special on the island?**
Fireworks at Saint-Martin-de-Ré on the evening of July 13 or 14, plus animated port. Worth staying for if you're on the island.
