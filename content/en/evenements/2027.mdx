Events and seasonal highlights on the Île de Ré in 2027.

## The annual programme

The island's event calendar follows a reliable annual structure: markets from April through October, summer festivals in July and August, the salt harvest in September-October, bird migration in spring and autumn.

## Planning for 2027

Specific programming will be confirmed by the island's tourism office closer to the date. The calendar reliably includes:
- Regular village markets (La Flotte, Saint-Martin, Ars)
- Summer musical events in Saint-Martin
- Bastille Day celebrations
- Salt harvest demonstrations at the Ecomuseum

## Booking accommodation around events

If a specific event drives your timing, book accommodation immediately once you have confirmed dates — popular event weekends fill quickly.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a good reason to time your 2027 visit around a specific event?**
The regular markets and seasonal activities are sufficient reason for most visitors. Specific festivals are a bonus rather than the primary draw.

**Where to check for 2027 events?**
The Île de Ré tourism office website (tourisme-iledere.com) is the authoritative source.
