Planning the budget for an Île de Ré holiday avoids surprises and helps choose the right timing and base.

## The main costs

**Bridge toll:** €16–19 each way in summer (technically one-way, as return is free).

**Accommodation:** The largest variable. See the accommodation pages for detailed price ranges.

**Food:** €30–50 per person per day for a mix of market, restaurant and self-catering.

**Bikes:** €10–35 per day depending on type.

**Activities and entry fees:** Lighthouse, museum, water sports — budget €100–200 per week for a family of 4.

## Budget strategies

Choose shoulder season (June or September). Base in La Couarde or Le Bois-Plage rather than Saint-Martin. Self-cater most meals using market produce. Standard bikes over electric.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Île de Ré significantly more expensive than other French Atlantic coasts?**
Yes. Prices for accommodation are above average for the Atlantic coast.

**Can you estimate a total budget for a family of 4 for a week?**
Base estimate: €3,500–5,000 total including accommodation, food, activities and travel. The accommodation cost is the dominant variable.
