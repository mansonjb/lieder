Reaching the Île de Ré by train is practical and comfortable — TGV to La Rochelle, then transfer to the island.

## The journey

**Paris Montparnasse → La Rochelle:** 3 hours on direct TGV. Multiple daily departures. Book in advance (2–4 weeks) for the best prices.

**La Rochelle → Île de Ré:** Lilas bus from the station (seasonal), taxi (€25–35 to Rivedoux), or joining a friend with a car. Cycle hire is available in La Rochelle for the bridge crossing.

## Trains from other cities

Nantes to La Rochelle: 1.5 hours. Bordeaux: 1.5 hours. Lyon: 3.5 hours. Toulouse: 3.5 hours.

## On the island without a car

The island's cycle path network means a car is not necessary once you arrive. Hire bikes on the island for the duration.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you bring a bike on the TGV to La Rochelle?**
Yes, with a reservation (mandatory). Check SNCF for current bike reservation rules.

**Is the TGV reliable?**
Generally excellent. Allow connection time in La Rochelle for the transfer to the island.
