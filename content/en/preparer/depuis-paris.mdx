Paris to the Île de Ré is a straightforward journey by multiple routes.

## By train + transfer

TGV from Paris Montparnasse to La Rochelle: 3 hours, multiple daily departures. From La Rochelle, taxi to the island (approximately €25–35 to Rivedoux) or bus (Lilas line, seasonal). Add 30–45 minutes from La Rochelle station to the island.

Total travel time: approximately 3.5–4 hours door to door.

## By car

Paris to Rivedoux via A10 motorway: approximately 4.5–5 hours in normal traffic. Add the bridge toll (€16–19 in peak season).

## By plane

No direct Paris to La Rochelle air route makes sense for this journey. Train is faster and more convenient.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is the train or car faster from Paris?**
Door-to-door, they're comparable. Train wins for those without specific need for a car on the island.

**Is there parking in La Rochelle to leave the car?**
Yes — La Rochelle has secure parking near the train station. Taking the bus from La Rochelle to the island avoids bridge traffic.
