Packing for the Île de Ré depends on the season, but some items are essential year-round.

## Always bring

- Sun protection (SPF 30+ minimum, high-SPF for children)
- A windproof layer — the Atlantic coast is breezy even on hot days
- Comfortable closed-toe shoes or sandals for cycling and walking on paths
- A water bottle for cycling days

## Summer additions

- Swimwear (obvious)
- A hat for sun protection
- Light evening layer — coastal evenings can be cool even in August

## For families

- Beach toys, snorkel sets
- Children's life jackets if kayaking (hire shops also provide)
- Insect repellent for marsh-side evenings

## Off-season additions

- Waterproof jacket
- Warm layers
- Wellies for marsh and beach walking

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere to buy forgotten items on the island?**
Yes — supermarkets in Saint-Martin, Sainte-Marie and Le Bois-Plage stock most essentials.

**Should I bring my own bike helmet?**
Children's helmets are worth bringing if you have a reliable one. Adult helmets are not legally required on cycle paths in France.
