Visiting the Île de Ré without a car is not only possible — for many visitors it's the better option.

## How to get there without a car

TGV to La Rochelle, then the Lilas bus to the island (seasonal, from La Rochelle train station). Or taxi from La Rochelle (€25–35 to Rivedoux).

## Getting around on the island

Hire a bike for the week. The cycle path network covers every village. This is genuinely the best way to experience the island.

## Accommodation

Choose a village with walkable services (bakery, market, restaurant). Saint-Martin, La Flotte, Le Bois-Plage and Sainte-Marie all work well.

## Shopping

Supermarkets are accessible by bike, including carrying supplies in pannier bags.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is a car-free Île de Ré holiday limiting?**
Not in terms of what you can see and do. A bike covers everything the island offers.

**What about luggage?**
Taxi from La Rochelle to your accommodation handles luggage on arrival. For the rest of the stay, your base is fixed.
