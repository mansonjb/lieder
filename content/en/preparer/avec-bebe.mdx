Visiting the Île de Ré with a baby requires some planning but the island is very well-suited to it.

## Essentials

Supermarkets in Saint-Martin, Sainte-Marie-de-Ré and Le Bois-Plage stock formula, nappies and baby food. Pharmacies in the main villages.

## Cycling with a baby

Baby trailers and front-seat attachments are available at most hire shops. The flat cycle paths make cycling with a trailer practical and comfortable.

## Beaches for babies

Sablanceaux (Rivedoux) and Les Grenettes (Sainte-Marie) are the ideal beaches for babies — very shallow, calm, warm water on sunny days.

## Accommodation

Confirm the property has a cot (lit bébé) when booking. Most rentals can provide one on request.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is the island manageable with a newborn?**
With careful planning, yes. The main practicalities (supplies, calm beaches) are in place. The island's pace is compatible with baby schedules.

**Is it worth hiring a bike with a baby?**
Yes — the trailer system is comfortable and the paths are smooth and flat.
