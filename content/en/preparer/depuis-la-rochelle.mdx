La Rochelle is the gateway to the Île de Ré — 20–30 minutes away by car or bus.

## By car

La Rochelle to Rivedoux-Plage via the bridge: 20 minutes in normal traffic, up to 60 minutes in summer peak. The bridge toll applies (€16–19 in summer, free to exit).

## By bus

The Lilas bus service runs between La Rochelle and the island's main villages, with greater frequency in summer. Seasonal schedule — check current timetables. Journey time: 45–60 minutes to Saint-Martin-de-Ré.

## By taxi

Approximately €25–35 from La Rochelle station to Rivedoux, more to villages further along the island.

## By bike

Cycling from La Rochelle is possible — the bridge has a dedicated cycle path. Allow 45–60 minutes from the city centre to Rivedoux.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a direct bus from La Rochelle airport to the island?**
Not a direct service. Taxi or connecting bus via La Rochelle centre.

**Can you store luggage in La Rochelle and take the bus to the island?**
Yes — La Rochelle station has left-luggage facilities.
