Parking on the Île de Ré is manageable with planning, and much less stressful if you don't use the car for beach excursions.

## Village parking

Saint-Martin-de-Ré and La Flotte have limited parking — paid and often full in summer. Arrive before 9am or after 6pm for best availability.

## Beach parking

Major Atlantic beaches (Le Gros Jonc, Les Gollandières) have car parks that fill by 10am in July-August. Pertuis beaches are more accessible.

## The solution

Park at your accommodation and use a bike. This eliminates the parking problem completely and is a better way to experience the island.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is parking free on the island?**
Some areas have free parking; others are paid. The situation changes by commune and season.

**Is there parking near the Phare des Baleines?**
Yes, a car park directly at the lighthouse. Gets busy; arrive early in summer.
