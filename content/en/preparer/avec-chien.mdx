Dogs are welcome on the Île de Ré with some seasonal restrictions, particularly around beach access.

## Beach restrictions

Dogs are banned from most beaches from May to September. Strictly enforced on supervised beaches in July-August. From October to April, dogs are generally welcome on most beaches on a lead.

## Where dogs are always welcome

Cycle paths, coastal walking paths away from beaches, village streets.

## Accommodation

Filter specifically for "animaux acceptés" (pets welcome) on rental platforms. Always confirm before booking.

## Practical tips

Bring water for your dog — beach days without shade and access to the water can be hot. Keep on a lead in the nature reserve.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is the island dog-friendly in summer?**
Less so than off-season, given beach restrictions. But the cycle paths and walking routes remain accessible.

**Are dogs allowed on the bridge?**
Yes — the pedestrian/cycle path permits dogs on leads.
