Two apps are particularly useful for getting the most from the Île de Ré: a weather app and a tide app.

## Weather

**Météo France** (official French meteorological service): most reliable for French Atlantic forecasts. Free app with hourly forecasts.

**Windfinder:** specifically useful for wind forecasts — important for kite, sail and surf planning on the island.

## Tides

**Marée.info:** the French reference for tide tables. Free, covers every coastal location, gives hourly times and heights. Essential for pêche à pied (shore fishing) planning.

## Why tides matter on Île de Ré

The tidal range is significant (up to 6m in spring tides). Beaches that are accessible at mid-tide are mudflats at high tide. Knowing what time the tide is is as useful as knowing the weather.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Google's weather forecast reliable enough for the island?**
Adequate for general planning. For wind-specific activities (kite, sail) use Windfinder.

**Do I need to know the tide coefficient?**
Useful for pêche à pied — coefficients above 90 give the best shore fishing conditions. Marée.info displays the coefficient alongside the times.
