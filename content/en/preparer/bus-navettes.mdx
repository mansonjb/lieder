The Lilas bus network provides public transport on the Île de Ré, connecting the main villages.

## The routes

The main routes run from the bridge (Rivedoux-Plage) along the island's main road to Saint-Martin-de-Ré. Some routes continue west. Summer schedule is more frequent than winter.

## Frequency

Summer: buses every 30–60 minutes on main routes. Winter: significantly reduced.

## Who it's for

Visitors without bikes who need to move between villages. Those arriving at Rivedoux by bus from La Rochelle.

## Limitations

The bus doesn't reach Loix, Les Portes or some of the more remote areas. For these, bike or car is necessary.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is the bus free on the island?**
Not free, but affordable. Check current fares on the Lilas website or at La Rochelle bus station.

**Can you rely on the bus for daily island exploration?**
It supplements but doesn't replace a bike. For full island access, hire a bike.
