Saint-Martin-de-Ré has the island's widest selection of hotels, making it the most practical base for anyone who doesn't want to self-cater.

## Hotels

The best-known hotel options on the island are in or near Saint-Martin. From small boutique hotels within the fortifications to larger properties on the outskirts. Quality is generally high; prices reflect it.

## Chambres d'hôtes

Several notable bed-and-breakfast properties in the town's historic fabric. Often the best combination of character and access.

## Holiday rentals

Available in quantity, though prices in Saint-Martin tend to be among the island's highest for equivalent space.

## Booking

Hotel rooms book faster here than anywhere else on the island. For July-August, book 4–6 months in advance. Some properties require minimum stays in peak season.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are hotels in Saint-Martin expensive?**
Among the most expensive on the island. The setting justifies it for many visitors; those on a budget may prefer La Flotte or Le Bois-Plage.

**Is Saint-Martin walkable from accommodation?**
Most accommodation in the town is within easy walking distance of port, shops and restaurants.
