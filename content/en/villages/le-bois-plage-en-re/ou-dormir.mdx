Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré has one of the best-stocked accommodation markets on the island, with strong camping provision and a wide range of rental properties.

## Campsites

Several well-equipped campsites, some close to the beaches. The island's camping offer is best here and in La Couarde. Facilities range from basic to very comfortable, with pools, restaurants and entertainment.

## Holiday rentals

Large stock of houses and apartments. Being a popular but not premium village, prices are a step below Saint-Martin and La Flotte for equivalent quality.

## Booking

Book early for July-August. Campsites book out as fast as houses — don't assume flexibility.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there beachfront properties in Le Bois-Plage?**
Some — but the Atlantic coast properties are more exposed. Check carefully what faces the beach vs. what's a short walk away.

**Is Le Bois-Plage cheaper than Saint-Martin?**
Generally yes, by 10–20% for equivalent space and dates.
