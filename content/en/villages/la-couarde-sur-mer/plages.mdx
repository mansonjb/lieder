La Couarde's double coastline gives visitors a choice that most island communes can't match — Atlantic open beach or calm pertuis water within minutes of each other.

## Atlantic beach (northern coast)

Exposed, windy, with some surf potential. Good for those who want an open-sea feel. Not recommended for very young children on rough days.

## Pertuis coast (southern coast)

Calm, shallow, ideal for families. The water is warmer than the Atlantic side and well-protected from wind.

## Choosing

By weather: check wind direction. A south-westerly pushes waves onto the Atlantic coast and leaves the pertuis calm. North-easterlies (rare in summer) do the opposite.

## Frequently asked questions

**Which La Couarde beach is supervised?**
The main Atlantic beach has supervision in July-August. Confirm locally for the pertuis side.

**How far is the pertuis beach from the village centre?**
About 5–10 minutes on foot or 3 minutes by bike.
