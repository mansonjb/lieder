La Couarde-sur-Mer has a decent restaurant selection for its size, with seasonal openings covering most needs.

## In-season options

Seafood restaurants, crêperies and casual dining. The market provides the best fresh produce for those who prefer to cook.

## Practicalities

Most restaurants operate only in season (June to September). Outside this period, choices narrow significantly.

## Nearby

La Flotte and Saint-Martin are both within 20 minutes by bike if you want more choice for a special dinner.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there good restaurants in La Couarde?**
Competent rather than outstanding. The island's best restaurants are concentrated in Saint-Martin and La Flotte.

**Is there a bakery?**
Yes, open year-round and early in the morning.
