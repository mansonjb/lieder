La Couarde-sur-Mer has a strong accommodation offer, particularly for families who want space and outdoor amenities.

## Campsites

Some of the island's best camping here, rivalling Le Bois-Plage in quality and volume. Well-equipped sites with pools, mobile homes and pitches.

## Holiday rentals

Good supply of houses with gardens. The village's family-holiday focus means many properties are well-suited for children.

## Booking

Book early for peak season. La Couarde is popular enough that July-August accommodation moves fast.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there hotels in La Couarde?**
Limited hotel offer — primarily rentals and campsites. For hotels, Saint-Martin or La Flotte are better.

**Is La Couarde good value compared to Saint-Martin?**
Generally better value for space. The village is less prestigious but very practical.
