Ars-en-Ré has a good selection of accommodation for a village of its size, with chambres d'hôtes and gîtes being the dominant offer.

## Chambres d'hôtes

Several notable bed-and-breakfast properties in the village's historic fabric. Often the best way to experience the village's character. Book early — demand exceeds supply.

## Holiday rentals

Houses and apartments in and around the village. Quality is generally high; Ars attracts visitors who appreciate its character, and properties tend to reflect this.

## Campsites

One well-established campsite near the village. More limited than the camping offer further east.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Ars-en-Ré good value compared to Saint-Martin?**
Generally better value for similar quality, given less peak demand. Prices are still above the island average due to the village's desirability.

**Is there a hotel in Ars-en-Ré?**
Limited hotel infrastructure. The village specialises in gîtes and chambres d'hôtes rather than hotels.
