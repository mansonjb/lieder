Ars-en-Ré has a small but good restaurant scene — consistently better value than Saint-Martin, with the same quality produce from the local salt marshes and nearby shellfish beds.

## What to expect

Simple, well-executed seafood. Fresh oysters. The market provides excellent raw materials. A few wine bars and crêperies complete the offer.

## The market

Friday morning in season at Ars is one of the island's best market experiences — smaller than Saint-Martin, more local, less crowded.

## Evening dining

Ars has enough restaurants for several good dinners during a week's stay. Reserve in advance for July-August weekends.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are Ars restaurants better value than Saint-Martin?**
Yes, typically. You pay less for comparable quality.

**Is there anywhere to eat oysters in Ars?**
Yes — the market and some restaurants serve local oysters. Ask for the current season's recommendation.
