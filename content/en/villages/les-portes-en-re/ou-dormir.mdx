Les Portes-en-Ré has very limited accommodation — a handful of gîtes, no hotel, no campsite. What's available books out early.

## What exists

Self-catering houses and gîtes in the village. Nothing else. Book at least 6 months ahead for July and August.

## The appeal

Staying in Les Portes means fully committing to the island's quietest commune. No pop-out options for dinner, no hotel bar. The reward is genuine remoteness and uncommercialised village life.

## Alternative

Les Portes as a day trip from Ars-en-Ré or Saint-Clément is a very reasonable option for those who want its beaches without basing there.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you stay in Les Portes for a week?**
Yes, if you find accommodation. Many who do report it as a defining trip experience. Bring enough of everything.

**Is there a supermarket in Les Portes?**
No. A small shop with basics. Do your main shopping in Ars or further east.
