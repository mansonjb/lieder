Les Portes-en-Ré has one or two seasonal restaurants — not a reliable dining scene.

## The reality

Calling ahead before visiting specifically for a meal is essential. Operating hours are unpredictable outside peak season.

## What to do instead

Picnic on the beach. Shop at the market in Ars-en-Ré (20 minutes by bike) and eat well without relying on open restaurants.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere to eat in Les Portes in September?**
Possibly. Confirm before going. Post-15 August the offer shrinks fast.

**Is there a bakery in Les Portes?**
Sometimes in season. A local bread delivery may operate — ask your accommodation host.
