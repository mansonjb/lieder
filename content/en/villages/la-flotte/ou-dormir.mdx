La Flotte has a quality accommodation offer that matches its reputation as one of the island's most desirable villages.

## Character accommodation

Chambres d'hôtes in converted stone houses, boutique rentals with character — La Flotte attracts visitors who care about where they stay, and the accommodation stock reflects this.

## What's available

Holiday rentals, chambres d'hôtes, and a small number of hotel-style properties. Not the most diverse offer in quantity, but high in average quality.

## Booking

High demand, especially for the better properties. Book 6–8 months ahead for July and August. June and September have more flexibility.

## Proximity advantage

From La Flotte you can walk to the beach, the port and the market. No car needed once you're there.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Flotte the most expensive village for accommodation?**
Among the pricier options. Saint-Martin tends to be similar or higher. La Flotte properties often justify their prices through character.

**Are there gîtes for large groups in La Flotte?**
Some. Search specifically for capacity. The village's compact nature means fewer large properties than the bigger communes.
