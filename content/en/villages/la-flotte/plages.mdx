La Flotte's main beach is sheltered, calm and a short walk from the port — making it one of the most convenient and pleasant for a town-based visitor.

## La Flotte beach

North-facing, on the pertuis side, protected from the prevailing south-westerly winds. Sand is fine, the gradient into the water is gentle, the setting is pleasant without drama. Supervised in season.

## Who it suits

Families with young children, anyone who wants a civilised beach within walking distance of lunch and dinner. Not a wave-sport beach.

## Access

Easily walkable from the port and village centre, about 5 minutes on foot. No car needed.

## Facilities

Supervised in July-August. Some seasonal amenities (showers, changing rooms) on site.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Flotte beach large?**
Medium-sized. It's comfortable without ever being empty in peak season.

**Is there parking near the beach?**
There's parking on the heights above the village, with a walk down to the port and beach. Arrive early in summer.
