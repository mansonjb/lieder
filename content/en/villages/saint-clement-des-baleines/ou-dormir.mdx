Saint-Clément-des-Baleines has limited accommodation — gîtes and rental properties in a quiet village with few visitors who stay, most passing through for the lighthouse.

## What's available

Self-catering gîtes and holiday rentals. No hotels. A genuinely quiet place to stay if you specifically want the western end of the island.

## Who it suits

Those who want the island's quietest atmosphere with the lighthouse as a nearby landmark. Better for independent travellers than those wanting services on the doorstep.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere to stay near the lighthouse?**
Some properties are within walking distance. Check platforms for exact locations — the village is small enough that nothing is very far.

**Is Ars-en-Ré a better base for this part of the island?**
For more services and atmosphere, yes. Ars is 15 minutes by bike and has more options.
