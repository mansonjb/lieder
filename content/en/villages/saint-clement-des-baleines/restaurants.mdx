Saint-Clément-des-Baleines has minimal restaurant infrastructure — a couple of seasonal options and nothing more.

## The offer

A seasonal restaurant or two in the village, mostly catering to lighthouse visitors passing through. Not a dining destination.

## Practical advice

If you're spending time in Saint-Clément, either bring a picnic or plan to cycle to Ars-en-Ré (15 minutes) for meals.

## Near the lighthouse

A snack bar operates near the lighthouse car park in peak season. Basic but convenient.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere to eat after visiting the lighthouse?**
A snack option near the car park in season. For a full meal, Ars-en-Ré is the nearest reliable option.
