Rivedoux-Plage has a practical range of accommodation — not the island's most characterful, but well-placed and well-priced compared to Saint-Martin.

## Types of accommodation

Holiday rentals are the dominant option. Houses and apartments with gardens, typically rented by the week. Prices are lower than the premium villages but still require early booking for summer.

A small number of chambres d'hôtes and serviced apartments operate year-round.

## Practical advantages

Being the first village after the bridge means easier access for late arrivals, more flexible car parking, and quick escapes if the weekend gets too busy.

## Booking

Use the Stay22 map to compare prices across Airbnb, Booking.com and Vrbo. For July-August, book by February at the latest.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there hotels in Rivedoux-Plage?**
Limited hotel offer. Most accommodation is in rentals or chambres d'hôtes.

**Is it cheaper to stay in Rivedoux than Saint-Martin?**
Generally yes, for equivalent space.
