Rivedoux has two main beaches on the calm, pertuis side of the island — ideal for families and anyone who wants relaxed, supervised swimming.

## Sablanceaux (Rivedoux Sud)

The main beach, long and sandy, supervised in July-August. Very calm water, gently shelving — excellent for young children. Kayak hire available in season.

## Rivedoux Nord

Slightly more exposed but still sheltered compared to the Atlantic side. Less busy than Sablanceaux.

## Bridge view

Both beaches offer a view of the bridge, which is either a feature or a distraction depending on your preference for unspoiled horizons.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there waves at Rivedoux beaches?**
Rarely. The pertuis side is protected and calm. For waves, head to the Atlantic beaches at Le Bois-Plage or La Couarde.

**Is parking easy at Rivedoux beach?**
Relatively good compared to the more popular beaches. Still best to arrive early in peak season.
