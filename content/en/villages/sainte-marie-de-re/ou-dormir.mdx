Sainte-Marie-de-Ré has the island's widest range of accommodation after Saint-Martin, with options at every budget level.

## Holiday rentals

The dominant form of accommodation. Large supply of houses and apartments, meaning slightly more availability than the most sought-after villages. Still book early for peak season.

## Campsites

Several well-equipped campsites near the village and beaches. Some of the island's best camping infrastructure is here.

## Hotels

A small number of hotels, mostly 2–3 star. More functional than characterful.

## Booking

The Stay22 map compares live rates across platforms. For July-August, book by January. Shoulder season (June, September) is more flexible.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Sainte-Marie good value compared to Saint-Martin?**
Generally yes. Similar convenience, lower prices on average.

**Are pets accepted in accommodation?**
Some properties accept pets. Filter specifically when searching.
