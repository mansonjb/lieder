Sainte-Marie-de-Ré has a good restaurant scene for an island commune, with enough variety to eat differently most nights of a week's stay.

## What to expect

Seafood is the main theme — fish of the day, oysters from the local beds, mussels, langoustines in season. A few crêperies and casual options for those who want lighter or cheaper meals.

## Best areas

The village centre and the areas near Les Grenettes beach have the highest concentration. Port-side dining, where available, gives the best atmosphere.

## Opening times

In season (July-August), most are open daily. Spring and autumn offer reduced days. Winter: very limited.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are restaurants in Sainte-Marie good value?**
Comparable to the island average. Lunch set menus are the best value, typically 15–22€.

**Are there restaurants for vegetarians?**
Increasingly yes. The island's fresh vegetable produce is excellent and features on most menus.
