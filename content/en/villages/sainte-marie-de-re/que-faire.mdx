Sainte-Marie-de-Ré is one of the best-served communes for activities on the island. Its size and range of beaches give more variety than most.

## Kitesurfing and windsurfing at La Cible

La Cible beach is the island's main kitesurfing hub. A school operates in season, and the consistent south-westerly wind makes it well-suited to learning and progression. Lessons available for all levels.

## Family beaches

Les Grenettes on the pertuis side is the ideal family beach — calm, shallow, supervised, with easy facilities nearby. Good conditions for kayaks and paddleboards.

## Markets

The market in Sainte-Marie is one of the island's better ones, with local produce and a good atmosphere in season.

## Cycling

Direct access to the main island cycle path in both directions. The route eastward toward Rivedoux and the bridge, and westward toward La Flotte and Saint-Martin.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a surf school in Sainte-Marie?**
Kitesurfing, yes. For surfing waves, Le Bois-Plage is the better base.

**Is there enough to do in Sainte-Marie for a week?**
Yes, combined with cycling excursions to the rest of the island.
