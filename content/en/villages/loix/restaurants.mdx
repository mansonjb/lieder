Loix has one of the smallest restaurant offers on the island. A single restaurant or two operates in season — check availability before making a trip specifically for dining.

## The reality

This is not a dining destination. The pleasure of Loix is its remoteness, and that comes with minimal services. If you're cycling through for lunch, check in advance that something is open.

## What to bring

A picnic from the market at Ars-en-Ré or La Couarde is a better bet than relying on finding an open restaurant in Loix. The marshes provide a perfect picnic setting.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there anywhere to eat in Loix in October?**
Very unlikely. By mid-September most seasonal establishments in Loix close.

**Is there a boulangerie in Loix?**
Minimal services year-round. Bring bread from a neighbouring village.
