Loix has very limited accommodation — a handful of gîtes and rental properties in the village and surrounding area, with nothing resembling a hotel.

## What's available

Self-catering gîtes, a small number of chambres d'hôtes. Supply is genuinely tight in a village this small.

## Who stays here

Those who have researched the island carefully and specifically want the quietest possible base. Often returning visitors.

## Booking

Book well in advance — what little is available fills early. Use Airbnb, Vrbo or local agencies. The Stay22 map may show limited options.

## Alternative

If accommodation in Loix is unavailable, Ars-en-Ré is close (20 minutes by bike) and has more options while offering similar access to the marshes and quiet atmosphere.

## Frequently asked questions

**Are there campsites in Loix?**
Not in the village itself. Nearby communes (La Couarde, Le Bois-Plage) have the island's main camping offer.

**Is staying in Loix worth the effort of finding accommodation?**
For those who want total quiet and authentic island life, yes absolutely.
