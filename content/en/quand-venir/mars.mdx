March is the island's transition month — still quiet at the start, but gathering momentum toward the tourist season by month's end.

## The season wakes up

By mid-March, some restaurants and shops begin to open. The flowering of the hollyhocks begins (though main bloom is June-August). Daylight extends noticeably — sunset is after 7:30pm by month's end.

## Bird migration

Spring migration begins in earnest — the reserve at Lilleau des Niges starts to see returning species from Africa. An excellent time for birdwatchers.

## Cycling

The cycle paths are empty. For those who enjoy cycling without traffic, March is an excellent month.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can you find somewhere to eat in March?**
Yes, but check in advance. March is still mostly quiet. By the last week, more options are opening.

**What's the weather like in March?**
Variable — can be cold and stormy, or mild and sunny. The Atlantic spring is unpredictable.
