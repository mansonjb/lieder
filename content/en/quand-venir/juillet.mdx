July is the island's peak month — everything is open, the weather is typically excellent, and the island is at full capacity.

## July character

Hot and sunny most days (24–27°C average). Sea temperature 20–21°C. All beaches supervised. Every restaurant, shop and service operating. Markets at maximum.

## Crowds

The island's population multiplies from 18,000 to over 100,000. Traffic on the main road, queues at the bridge, packed beaches. This is real — plan accordingly.

## What's still worth it

The quality of the experience at its peak. The evening atmosphere. The markets. The specific summer energy that only exists in this month.

## Booking

If you haven't booked July accommodation by February, your options are severely limited.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is July too crowded to enjoy?**
It depends on your crowd tolerance. The island manages its peak well — beaches are big, cycle paths are wide. But it is genuinely busy.

**What's the best time of day in July to avoid crowds?**
Early morning (before 10am) for beaches, markets and villages. Late evening (after 8pm) for restaurants.
