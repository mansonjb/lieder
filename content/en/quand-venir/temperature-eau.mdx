Sea temperature on the Île de Ré varies through the year from around 8°C in February to 22°C in August.

## Monthly guide

| Month | Approx. temperature |
|---|---|
| January | 8–9°C |
| February | 8–9°C |
| March | 9–10°C |
| April | 11–13°C |
| May | 14–16°C |
| June | 16–18°C |
| July | 19–21°C |
| August | 21–22°C |
| September | 19–21°C |
| October | 17–18°C |
| November | 13–15°C |
| December | 10–12°C |

## The two coasts

Atlantic side: generally 1–2°C cooler than the pertuis side, due to deeper water and more direct ocean exposure. In summer, both are comfortable for swimming.

## Frequently asked questions

**When is the sea warmest?**
August peaks at 21–22°C on the pertuis side. September is almost as warm.

**Do people swim with wetsuits in June?**
Some do. A 3/2mm wetsuit makes June very comfortable. Without one, 16–18°C is brisk but manageable for most adults.
