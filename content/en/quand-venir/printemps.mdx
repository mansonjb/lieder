Spring (April-June) is one of the island's best seasons — flowers, birds, warming weather and uncrowded cycle paths.

## The spring arc

**April:** Season opens, flowers begin, some swimming for the brave.
**May:** AOP potatoes arrive, full services open, the island is green and uncrowded.
**June:** Best of spring-summer. Warm, long days, water good for swimming.

## Why spring works

No crowds. Fresh produce arriving in the markets. The hollyhocks begin their climb up the stone walls. Bird migration at its best in April-May.

## Spring value

Prices are 30–50% below summer peak. Good availability across all accommodation types.

## Frequently asked questions

**When is the earliest you can comfortably swim on Île de Ré?**
For hardy swimmers: May. For most people: June, when water reaches 16–18°C.

**When do hollyhocks bloom?**
They begin in May-June, reaching full bloom in late June through August.
