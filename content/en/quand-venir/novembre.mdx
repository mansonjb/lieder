November on the Île de Ré is full off-season — the island is at its quietest and most authentic, beautiful in a stark Atlantic way.

## November character

Cold (10–13°C), often windy, beautiful light in clear spells. The marshes are atmospheric in the winter palette. Very few visitors.

## What's open

A limited set of year-round businesses in Saint-Martin. Some restaurants in the main villages. Most seasonal infrastructure closed.

## Who visits in November

Walkers, birdwatchers, painters, those who know the island well and seek its off-season character.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is November a good month to visit if you want a peaceful experience?**
Yes — genuinely peaceful. Not for beach holidays.

**Is there anything going on in November on the island?**
Quiet cultural events in Saint-Martin sometimes. No major tourist programming.
