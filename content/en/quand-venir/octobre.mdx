October marks the transition to the island's authentic off-season. The tourist infrastructure reduces but the island itself becomes more genuinely itself.

## October character

Days shortening, temperature dropping (16–19°C), some stormy spells. The salt harvest happens in October — one of the genuine seasonal events worth seeing.

## The salt harvest

Paludiers collect their sel gris (coarse grey salt) in October before the winter rains arrive. This is the volume harvest that follows the fleur de sel season. Watching it is one of the island's most distinctive autumn experiences.

## Services

About half the tourist infrastructure closes during October. A good selection of restaurants and shops remains in Saint-Martin; less elsewhere.

## Bird watching

Excellent — autumn migration is in full swing through the Lilleau des Niges reserve.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is October worth visiting for the salt harvest?**
Yes, if you specifically want to see the harvest. The landscape and light are also beautiful in their October mode.

**Is Île de Ré pleasant to visit in rainy October?**
Can be atmospheric. The wild beaches are dramatic in a storm. But plan for indoor alternatives.
