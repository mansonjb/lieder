February on the Île de Ré is similar to January — quiet, local, not a tourist destination. But days are lengthening and the first flowers appear by the end of the month.

## February character

Still cold, fewer storms than January on average. The first mild days can appear. The salt marshes and bird reserve are at their most abundant with wintering species.

## Opening situation

Very limited. Plan for self-catering. Some bakeries and essential shops are open; most tourist infrastructure remains closed until Easter.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there any tourist infrastructure in February?**
Minimal. This is the island's quietest month.

**When does the island start coming back to life?**
Easter marks the beginning. Some businesses reopen from April 1 onward.
