April is when the island comes alive — the tourist season properly begins, flowers appear, and the first genuinely warm days arrive.

## The season opening

Most restaurants and shops open for Easter, which falls in April most years. The market rhythm picks up. Cycle hire shops open fully. The island is accessible with almost full services by the second half of April.

## The beach situation

The Atlantic water is still cold (13–15°C) — too cold for most swimmers, but pleasant for walking. The calmer pertuis beaches are more comfortable on mild days.

## Hollyhocks

The island's famous roses trémières begin to show by late April and reach full bloom in June.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is April a good time to visit?**
Excellent for cycling, nature and avoiding crowds. The beach situation is limited for swimming but pleasant for walks.

**Are all restaurants open in April?**
Most by mid-April. Some smaller establishments wait until May or June.
