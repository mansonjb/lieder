A quiet pertuis option, good for those who prefer a more local beach experience without facilities.

## Location and character

A small beach on the island's pertuis coast, sheltered and calm. Relatively quiet and local in character.

## Access

By bike from the nearest village. Look for local beach signs.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Pergola a family beach?**
It's calm and sheltered, which suits families, though facilities are minimal.

**Is it supervised?**
No lifeguard cover at this beach.
