Quiet and less visited than the main beaches. Good for those who prefer uncrowded settings.

## Location and character

A small Atlantic or pertuis-side beach depending on location, with the natural character of the island's quieter stretches of coastline.

## Access

By bike from the nearest village. Not heavily signposted — local knowledge or a good map helps.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Petit Sergent often busy?**
Rarely. Its smaller scale and relative obscurity keep it quieter than the main beaches.

**Is it good for swimming?**
Yes in calm conditions. Always check the context — Atlantic-facing beaches require more judgement.
