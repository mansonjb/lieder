A local beach rather than a tourist destination. Relaxed atmosphere, no facilities.

## Location and character

A beach on the island's coast with natural character and limited development. Part of the quieter coastal stretch away from the main tourist beaches.

## Access

By bike from the nearest village.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Marchais crowded?**
No — one of the island's less visited beaches.

**Is it suitable for swimming?**
Yes in calm conditions. Check the coast orientation and conditions before entering.
