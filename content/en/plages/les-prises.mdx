Better for birdwatching and walking than swimming. The low-tide mudflats here are part of the Lilleau des Niges reserve.

## Location and character

A stretch of coastline on the northern pertuis coast of the island, in the area near Ars-en-Ré and the Lilleau des Niges reserve. More marsh and mudflat than sandy beach — excellent for nature observation.

## Access

By bike from Ars-en-Ré, near the entrance to the nature reserve.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Les Prises a swimming beach?**
Not primarily. It's more coastal nature zone than sandy beach.

**What's the birdlife like here?**
Excellent — the area is part of the Lilleau des Niges reserve, one of France's most important migratory bird sites.
