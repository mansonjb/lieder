A pertuis-side option for those who want calm water without the crowds of Sablanceaux or Les Grenettes.

## Location and character

A small, quiet beach on the southern pertuis coast of the island, accessible from the village network. Calmer water, natural setting, less infrastructure than the main family beaches.

## Access

By bike from the nearest village. Not the most signposted beach — local knowledge helps.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Arnerault beach supervised?**
No — it's a smaller, quieter option without lifeguard cover.

**What type of seabed?**
Sandy, typical of the pertuis coast. Calm and suitable for swimming in good conditions.
