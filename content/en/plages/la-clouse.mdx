One of the smaller, quieter beaches on the pertuis side. Good for those who want to avoid the crowds at the main family beaches.

## Location and character

A small beach on the island's southern coast, in the pertuis zone. Calm water, quiet setting, limited facilities.

## Access

By bike from the nearest village. Less signposted than main beaches.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Clouse supervised?**
No — small and quiet, without lifeguard cover.

**Is it good for swimming?**
Yes, calm pertuis conditions make it suitable for relaxed swimming.
