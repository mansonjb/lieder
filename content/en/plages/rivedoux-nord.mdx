A gentler alternative to the busier Sablanceaux. Good for swimming, paddling and kayaking. Supervised in peak season.

## Location and character

The northern beach at Rivedoux-Plage, on the pertuis side, slightly more exposed than Sablanceaux but still calm and sheltered compared to the Atlantic coast. Fine sand, reasonable depth.

## Access

Short walk from Rivedoux village centre. Cycle path access. Parking available nearby.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Rivedoux Nord less busy than Sablanceaux?**
Generally yes. It attracts fewer visitors and can feel more relaxed even in August.

**Is the water calm here?**
Yes — pertuis side, protected from the Atlantic swell.
