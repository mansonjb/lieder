Calm, sheltered and quiet. A pertuis beach with the typical characteristics of the eastern coastline.

## Location and character

A beach on the island's southern coast, pertuis side, with calm water and a natural setting. Named for a historical coastal defence in the area.

## Access

By bike from the nearest village. Coastal path.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Redoute a good swimming beach?**
Yes — pertuis side means calm water suitable for relaxed swimming.

**Is there any history at this location?**
The name references a coastal redoubt (small fortification) in the area, reflecting the island's long military history.
