Good for a quiet stop on a cycling day rather than a full beach day with all facilities.

## Location and character

One of the smaller beaches on the island, accessible from the cycle network. Natural character with limited development.

## Access

By bike from the nearest village. Not heavily signed.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Bénaie suitable for swimming?**
Yes in calm conditions — check which coast it faces before visiting.

**Are there any facilities?**
Minimal. Bring what you need.
