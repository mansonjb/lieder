The Île de Ré's consistent Atlantic wind makes it one of the better spots for kitesurfing and windsurfing on France's western coast.

## The main spot: La Cible

La Cible, on the Sainte-Marie-de-Ré commune, is the reference spot. Regular wind (predominant south-westerlies), shallow water over a large area — ideal for learning and progression. A kite school operates in season.

## Other spots

The Atlantic coast between Le Bois-Plage and La Couarde offers interesting conditions in strong winds. Less organised than La Cible but used by experienced riders.

## Equipment and lessons

Hire and lessons available at La Cible. Prices vary by level and duration.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Cible suitable for beginners?**
Yes — shallow water and consistent wind make it well-suited to beginners. Lessons and progressions are available.

**Is the wind reliable year-round?**
More consistent in shoulder season. July-August can have light wind periods, though breezy days occur throughout summer.
