La Patache is one of the westernmost beaches accessible from Les Portes-en-Ré — isolated, undeveloped and genuinely wild.

## Character

Similar to Gros Jonc at Les Portes, but slightly further along the coast. Very few visitors relative to its scale. Atlantic-facing, backed by dunes, no facilities.

## Who it's for

Walkers, birdwatchers, those seeking to be genuinely alone on a beach. Kitesurfers in the right wind conditions.

## Access

Beyond Gros Jonc from Les Portes, on a continuation of the dune track. Navigating requires some orientation.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Patache harder to access than Gros Jonc?**
Slightly further along the coast, requiring more cycling or walking from the village.

**Is there anything near La Patache?**
No. It's a remote beach with nothing on site or nearby.
