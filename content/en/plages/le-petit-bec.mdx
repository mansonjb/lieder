One of the quieter beach options, appreciated for its calm setting and lack of crowds.

## Location and character

A smaller beach on the island's coastline, with natural character and limited tourist infrastructure.

## Access

Accessible by bike from the nearest village. Check local signage.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Petit Bec a good swimming beach?**
Yes, conditions permitting. Check the coast it's on — pertuis for calm, Atlantic for waves.

**Is it supervised?**
Not a supervised beach. Take the usual precautions for unsupervised swimming.
