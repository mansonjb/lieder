Le Gros Jonc at Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré is one of the island's premier Atlantic beaches — large, consistent and with good surf conditions.

## Character

Wide, open, Atlantic-facing with a solid beach break. The scale is impressive — plenty of room even in August. Backed by dunes with access paths from the village and cycle paths.

## Surf

One of the island's main surf spots. Surf school present in season. Suitable for beginners to intermediate level. Consistent swell in autumn and spring; calmer in summer.

## Facilities

Supervision in season, showers and toilets, some seasonal catering. One of the island's more developed wild beaches.

## Access

From Le Bois-Plage village, about 15 minutes on foot or 5 minutes by bike. Cycle path access.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Gros Jonc supervised?**
Yes, in July-August. One of the few Atlantic beaches with supervision.

**Is Le Gros Jonc good for families?**
For older children who can manage some wave energy, yes. Very young children are better at the pertuis-side beaches.
