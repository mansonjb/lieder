One of the island's less-visited beach spots, appreciated by those who prefer to avoid crowds.

## Location and character

A small beach on the island's coast, quiet and local in character.

## Access

Accessible by bike from the nearest village.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Loge well-known?**
No — it's one of the island's quieter, more local beaches.

**Is it suitable for swimming?**
Yes in calm conditions. Check the coast it faces.
