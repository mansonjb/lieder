More a nature spot than a swimming destination. The point and its surroundings are interesting at low tide for marine life exploration.

## Location and character

A coastal point rather than a pure beach — rocky headland terrain gives way to sandy sections on the pertuis coast. Good for fishing and shellfish gathering at low tide.

## Access

By bike. Coastal path access from the nearest village.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Grouin good for swimming?**
Less so than the sandy beaches. More suited to walking, fishing and low-tide exploration.

**Is there shellfish gathering here?**
Yes — the rocky and sandy terrain at low tide can yield periwinkles and small crabs.
