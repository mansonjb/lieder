One of the island's best spots for shore fishing (cockles, periwinkles, small crabs) at low tide. The setting — surrounded by marshes and the remote village of Loix — is distinctive.

## Location and character

The small beach-cove at Loix, on the pertuis side. More famous for shellfish gathering at low tide than for swimming. Wide mudflats reveal at low water.

## Access

From Loix village, a short walk or cycle to the cove.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Boutillon good for shellfish gathering?**
Yes — one of the island's better spots for cockles and periwinkles at low tide. Check the tidal coefficient before going.

**Is it a swimming beach?**
It can be in calm conditions. It's better known as a low-tide exploration spot than a swimming beach.
