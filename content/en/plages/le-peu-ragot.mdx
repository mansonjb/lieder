For those who want the Le Bois-Plage Atlantic experience with fewer people. A similar character to Le Gros Jonc but quieter.

## Location and character

An Atlantic beach at Le Bois-Plage-en-Ré, wild and less visited than the more famous Le Gros Jonc nearby. Dune-backed, consistent swell, no facilities.

## Access

By bike from Le Bois-Plage village. Dune track access.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is Le Peu Ragot supervised?**
No. Unlike Le Gros Jonc, this is unsupervised. Experienced swimmers only in rough conditions.

**Is it a surf spot?**
Yes — beach break similar to Le Gros Jonc but less crowded.
