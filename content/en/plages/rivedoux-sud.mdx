One of the island's most family-friendly beaches. Calm, supervised in season, accessible facilities. Kayak hire available.

## Location and character

The main beach at Rivedoux-Plage, located immediately south of the bridge landing point. Calm, very shallow at low tide, with a view back toward the bridge and mainland.

## Access

A short walk from Rivedoux village. Easy parking compared to more popular beaches. Cycle path access from the bridge.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is this the first beach after the bridge?**
Yes — it's the most immediately accessible beach when arriving by car from La Rochelle.

**Is it shallow enough for toddlers?**
Very shallow at low tide — one of the best beaches on the island for very young children.
