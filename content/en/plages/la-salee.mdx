More a coastal nature area than a developed beach. The transition between salt marsh and sea is ecologically interesting.

## Location and character

A stretch of coastline on the pertuis side of the island, with the flat, open character typical of the eastern shore. Salt marsh transitions here.

## Access

By bike along the coastal path. The terrain is flat and the cycling is easy.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is La Salée a swimming beach?**
More of a nature area than a classic sandy beach. The setting is beautiful but not the island's prime swimming spot.

**Is there wildlife here?**
Yes — the salt marsh transition zone attracts waders and other birds.
